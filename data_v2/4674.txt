dardanelles to prevent german austro hungarian and turkish warships from breaking out into mediterranean she did however participate in attempt to ensure greek acquiescence to allied operations in macedonia in late 1916 mirabeau briefly participated in occupation of constantinople after end of war and was deployed in black sea in early 1919 during allied intervention in russian civil war she ran aground in february 1919 off coast of crimea and could not be refloated until some of her guns and armor were removed after returning to france ship was used as an accommodation hulk until she was
