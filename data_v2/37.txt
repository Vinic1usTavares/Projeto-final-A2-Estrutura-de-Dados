julius schreck 13 july 1898 16 may 1936 was an early senior nazi official and close confidant of adolf hitler born in munich schreck served in world war i and shortly afterwards joined right wing paramilitary units he joined nazi party in 1920 and developed a close friendship with adolf hitler schreck was a founding member of sturmabteilung storm detachment sa and was active in its development later in 1925 he became first leader of schutzstaffel protection squadron ss he then served for a time as a chauffeur for hitler schreck developed meningitis in 1936 and died on 16 may of that year hitler gave him a state funeral which was attended by several members of nazi elite with hitler delivering eulogy early life julius schreck was born on 13 july 1898 in munich in bavaria he served in german army during world war i after war ended in november 1918 he became a member of freikorps epp a right wing paramilitary unit formed to combat communistic revolution schreck was an early member career in sa schreck was a founding member of sturmabteilung storm detachment sa being involved in its growth and development this was a paramilitary wing of party designed to disrupt political opponents and provide muscle for security tasks hitler in early 1923 ordered formation of a small separate bodyguard dedicated to his service and protection rather than an uncontrolled mass of party
