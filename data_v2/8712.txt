son of neptune is a 2011 fantasy adventure novel written by american author rick riordan based on greek and roman mythology it is second book in heroes of olympus series preceded by lost hero and followed by mark of athena story follows adventures of amnesiac percy jackson a demigod son of poseidon as he meets a camp of roman demigods and goes to alaska with his new friends hazel levesque and frank zhang to free greek god of death thanatos and help save world from gaea earth goddess novel is narrated in third person switching between points of view of percy frank and hazel book received mostly critical acclaim won goodreads choice award in 2011 and appeared on several bestseller lists son of neptune was first published in hardcover on october 4 2011 by disney hyperion with a cover designed by illustrator john rocco after an initial hardcover printing of three million copies book has since been released in paperback as well as an audiobook and e book and has been translated into 37 languages development in an interview by scholastic with rick riordan for lost hero riordan was questioned about whereabouts of percy jackson author hinted that answer would be revealed as jason s quest progressed by end of book he said that readers would have a good idea where second book is going on may 26 2011 riordan released both cover art and first chapter for son of neptune confirming that percy would play a role in book on august 8 2011 rick riordan released a video giving more information about book and its characters video includes pictures of a black haired boy with a bow and arrow in his hands who is later revealed to be frank zhang a blonde haired boy holding a teddy bear later revealed as octavian a girl with black hair wearing gold armor and a purple cloak sitting on a
