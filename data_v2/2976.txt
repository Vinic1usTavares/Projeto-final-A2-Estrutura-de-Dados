vaughn samuel buddy alliston jr born december 14 1933 is a former professional american football player who played positions of guard and linebacker he played college football at university of mississippi before being drafted by green bay packers of national football league nfl in 15th round of 1956 nfl draft alliston first played professionally in canadian football league cfl with winnipeg blue bombers in 1956 he was runner up for cfl s most outstanding lineman award after years of military service he played in american football league afl for denver broncos in 1960 early career alliston played high school football at florence high school as a fullback he also contributed on special teams blocking 21 punts over course of his high school career in 1951 he blocked five punts in a single game in college he played for ole miss rebels from 1952 1955 as a rookie in 1952 alliston played as a guard on ole miss rookie b team he went on to play as both a left and right guard for rebels in following seasons in 1954 alliston became known as a quick player who was able to easily tackle opponents on defensive side of ball by his senior season ole miss head coach johnny vaught considered alliston to be a contender for all american honors and associated press expected him to be one of best guards of 1955 associated press went on recognize alliston several times throughout season on their sec checklist of stars today at conclusion of season alliston received a variety of honors including second team all sec and an honorable mention on all american team he was also selected as an honorable mention for both best offensive and best defensive guard in his conference among a poll of sec coaches he was voted colonel rebel by ole miss student body in december 1955 ole miss rebels defeated tcu horned frogs 14 13 in 1956 cotton bowl classic with alliston being named best lineman of game professional career green bay packers of national football league selected alliston in 15th round of 1956 nfl draft with 176th overall pick but he didn t play for packers instead alliston played for winnipeg blue bombers of canadian football league in all sixteen regular season games during their 1956 season recording two interceptions and a fumble recovery he was named a western interprovincial football union all star at offensive guard position during his rookie season alliston was also named a runner up for cfl s most outstanding lineman award after his rookie season in cfl he took a break from professional football to join united states air force while with air force he was a player on eglin air force base team which won 1958 shrimp bowl against brooke army medical center alliston rejoined blue bombers in 1959 but played in only one game in 1960 alliston initially joined oakland raiders in their training camp before moving to denver broncos alliston played in eleven regular season games for broncos as a linebacker he retired after 1960 season
