new york state route 31e ny 31e is a state highway located in western new york in united states it serves as a northerly alternate route of ny 31 between village of middleport in eastern niagara county and nearby village of medina in orleans county ny 31e connects to ny 31 at each end by way of a short overlap with ny 271 in middleport most of ny 31e parallels erie canal and parts of route run adjacent to waterway outside of two villages at each end ny 31e traverses mostly rural areas of towns of royalton and ridgeway origins of route date back to 1914 when its routing was included as part of a spur route of route 30 a cross state unsigned legislative route spur route was eliminated in 1921 and road went unnumbered until 1930 renumbering of state highways in new york when it became part of ny 3 most of ny 3 west of rochester was replaced by ny 31 in mid 1930s and ny 31 was realigned onto its present more southerly alignment between middleport and medina in 1949 its former alignment became ny 31e at this time most of ny 31e is maintained by new york state department of transportation nysdot however two sections in niagara county and medina are locally maintained route
