catedral de nuestra senora de guadalupe english cathedral of our lady of guadalupe or simply ponce cathedral is cathedral for roman catholic diocese of ponce located in downtown ponce puerto rico cathedral lies in middle of ponce s town square known as plaza las delicias located at center of ponce historic zone for its historic significance cathedral was listed on national register of historic places in 1984 it is seat of bishop of ponce currently ruben gonzalez medina bishop ruben antonio gonzalez medina c m f david m cheney catholic hierarchy org v3 1 2 25 jan 2016 date 4 february 2016
