tao li born 10 january 1990 is a singaporean competitive swimmer who specializes in backstroke and butterfly tao has represented singapore at southeast asian games sea games asian games world championship commonwealth games and olympics she holds several national records in fact she s only female singapore swimmer who qualified for an olympic finals at 2005 sea games in philippines at just 15 she won 4 golds and a bronze she reached finals of women s 50 metres 100 metres and 200 metres butterfly events at 2006 commonwealth games in melbourne though she did not win any medal in december 2006 tao won gold at 50 metres butterfly in doha s 2006 asian games beating china s multi gold medallist xu yanwei and breaking her own national record in process she was singapore s most medalled athlete at 2007 sea games in nakhon ratchasima korat thailand winning four gold medals earlier in year she became first singaporean female swimmer to reach a fina world aquatics championships final finishing seventh in 50 metres butterfly tao made her olympic debut for singapore at 2008 summer olympics in beijing she qualified in 100 metres finals ranking fourth in semifinals with a time of 57 54 seconds and setting two asian records in two days she thus became first singaporean swimmer to qualify in an olympic final she emerged fifth at women s 100 metres butterfly finals with a time of 57 99 seconds early life and education tao li was born on 10 january 1990 in wuhan hubei in people s republic of china only child of tao ran and his wife li yan who were both former provincial swimmers
