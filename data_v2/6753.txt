captain gurbachan singh salaria pvc 29 november 1935 5 december 1961 was an indian army officer and member of a united nations peacekeeping force singh was an alumnus of rashtriya military school bangalore king george s royal indian military college and national defence academy he was first nda alumnus and is only un peacekeeper to be awarded a param vir chakra pvc india s highest wartime military decoration in december 1961 salaria was among indian troops deployed to republic of congo as part of united nations operation in congo on 5 december salaria s battalion was tasked to clear a roadblock of two armoured cars manned by 150 gendarmes of secessionist state of katanga on way to elizabethville airport plan was that salaria and his men were to block their retreat his rocket launcher team attacked and destroyed katangese armoured cars this unforeseen move confused gendarmerie and salaria felt it would be best to attack before they reorganised though his troops were badly outnumbered they charged towards katangese and killed 40 men in a kukri assault during attack salaria was shot twice in neck and eventually succumbed to his injuries remaining gendarmes fled in utter confusion leaving their dead and wounded behind this helped main battalion to easily overrun katangese and clear roadblock for his duty and courage and disregard for his own safety during battle salaria was awarded param vir chakra early life and education gurbachan singh salaria was born on 29 november 1935 in a village near shakargarh punjab british india now in pakistan he was second of five children of munshi ram and dhan devi his father had earlier been drafted into dogra squadron of hodson s horse in british indian army listening to tales of his father and his regiment motivated salaria to join army at a very young age as a result of partition of india salaria s family moved to indian part of punjab and settled in village in district salaria enrolled in local village school and later in 1946 he was admitted to king george s royal indian military college bangalore rashtriya military school bangalore in august 1947 he was transferred to kgrimc in jalandhar after passing out from kgrimc he joined joint services wing of national defence academy nda on graduating from nda in 1956 he enrolled in indian military academy completing his studies on 9 june 1957 salaria was initially commissioned into 2nd battalion 3 gorkha rifles but was later transferred to 3rd battalion 1 gorkha rifles in march 1960 congo crisis in june 1960 republic of congo became independent from belgium but during first week of july a mutiny broke out in congolese army and violence erupted between black and white civilians belgium sent troops to protect fleeing whites and two areas of country katanga and south kasai subsequently seceded with belgian support congolese government asked united nations un for help and on 14 july 1960 organisation responded by establishing united nations operation in congo a large multi national peacekeeping force and aid mission between march june 1961 under command of brigadier k a s raja india contributed 99th infantry brigade around 3 000 men to un force after attempts at reconciliation between congolese government and katanga failed on 24 november un security council approved un security council resolution 169 resolution condemned katanga s secession and authorised use of force to immediately resolve conflict and establish peace in region in response katangese gendarmerie took two senior un officials hostage they were later released but major ajit singh of 1 gorkha rifles was also captured and eventually killed as was his driver roadblocks were erected by gendarmes to prevent communication between un detachments and isolating them from each other making it easier to take down detachments one by one on 4 december a block was established on road between city of elisabethville and nearby airport un troops reacted by initiating operation unokat to defend their positions and reestablish their freedom of movement in region operation unokat on 5 december 1961 3rd battalion 1 gorkha rifles was tasked to clear roadblock on way to elizabethville airport at a strategic roundabout roadblock was held by 150 gendarmes with two armoured cars plan was for first attack to be made by charlie company led by major govind sharma captain salaria with a platoon from alpha company close to airport road was to block gendarmes retreat and to attack them if required rest of alpha company was kept in reserve plan was to be executed at midday captain salaria and his troops reached specified location with their armoured personnel carriers they were positioned around from target his rocket launcher team was soon able to get close enough to gendarmes armoured cars to destroy them this unforeseen move left katangese confused and disorganised salaria felt that it was prudent to attack before gendarmes reorganised his last words over radio to another officer were i am going in for attack i am certain i will win though his troops were heavily outnumbered by gendarmes he charged towards them engaging in a hand to hand kukri assault whilst shouting gorkha war cry ayo salaria and his men killed 40 gendarmes but he was shot twice in neck by automatic gunfire he collapsed due to blood loss at last line of trenches his second in command was ordered to evacuate him in an armoured personnel carrier to airport hospital as soon as possible regardless salaria succumbed to his injuries close engagement with indian troops resulted in gendarmerie losing about half their men they fled in utter confusion leaving their dead and injured behind this enabled main battalion to easily overrun katangese force clear roadblock and prevent gendarmes from encircling un headquarters in elisabethville for his duty and courage and disregard for his own safety salaria was awarded param vir chakra param vir chakra for his gallant
