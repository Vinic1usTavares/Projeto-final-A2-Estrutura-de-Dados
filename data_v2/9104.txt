boccalandro in women s singles she came in 28th place out of 29 competitors background venezuela joined olympic competition at 1948 summer olympics and has participated in every summer olympic games since then 1998 nagano games were country s first appearance at a winter olympic games venezuela has as of 2018 won 15 medals at summer olympics but never won a medal at winter olympics these winter
