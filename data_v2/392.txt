grindcore is an extreme fusion genre of heavy metal and hardcore punk that originated in mid 1980s drawing inspiration from abrasive sounding musical styles such as thrashcore crust punk hardcore punk extreme metal and industrial grindcore is characterized by a noise filled sound that uses heavily distorted down tuned guitars grinding overdriven bass high speed tempo blast beats and vocals which
