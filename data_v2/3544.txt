shahid is a 2013 indian hindi language biographical drama film directed by hansal mehta jointly produced by anurag kashyap utv spotboy and sunil bohra it is based on life of lawyer and human rights activist shahid azmi who was assassinated in 2010 film stars rajkummar rao in titular role mohammed zeeshan ayyub prabhleen sandhu and baljinder kaur appear in supporting roles mehta took a break after some of his films were box office failures when he heard about azmi s murder he felt need to make a film about his life he began his research for film nearly three months after azmi s murder mehta met writer sameer gautam singh who wrote screenplay after researching azmi s life and spending months interviewing azmi s family apurva asrani did additional work on screenplay with mehta and also served as film s editor anuj dhawan was director of photography shahid had its world premiere at 2012 toronto international film festival s city to city programme in 2012 it was screened at several other film festivals including 14th mumbai film festival new york indian film festival indian film festival of stuttgart dubai international film festival and chicago south asian film festival film was awarded silver gateway trophy at mumbai film festival while mehta won best director award mehta also won best director award at new york indian film festival and at indian film festival of stuttgart it was released theatrically in india on 18 october 2013 and met with critical acclaim rao received best actor award and mehta best director award at 61st national film awards ceremony plot shahid azmi and his family are terrorised during bombay riots when hundreds of hindus and muslims died later he goes to kashmir and spends a brief period at a terror training camp but soon returns after witnessing an execution he moves back to mumbai where he is arrested under terrorist and disruptive activities prevention act for an alleged conspiracy to assassinate some politicians his brother arif azmi tries to bail him out but fails shahid is tortured by police and forced to admit to crime after which he spends seven years at tihar jail in new delhi during his incarceration omar sheikh tries to brainwash him another inmate ghulam navi waar inspires him to begin his college education and once he is acquitted of charges he studies law in mumbai after receiving his law degree shahid works with lawyer maqbool memon for a few months before starting his career as an independent lawyer with financial support of his elder brother arif during this process he meets mariam a muslim divorcee and marries her shahid soon starts picking up cases of muslims charged under prevention of terrorism act pota he fights many cases pro bono in consultation with non governmental organizations shahid s first major success as a defence lawyer is winning an acquittal due to lack of evidence for arif who was arrested under pota for 2002 mumbai bus bombing shahid represents accused in 2006 mumbai train bombings 2006 aurangabad arms haul and 2006 malegaon bombings case he is criticised for supporting terrorists and starts getting threatening calls that disturb his family life on one occasion shahid s face is blackened by assailants outside court room while defending faheem ansari in 2008 mumbai attacks case he is shot by two gunmen in his office and dies on spot later ansari is shown to be acquitted of all charges by supreme court of india due to lack of evidence cast rajkummar rao as shahid azmi mohammed zeeshan ayyub as arif azmi tigmanshu dhulia as maqbool memon
