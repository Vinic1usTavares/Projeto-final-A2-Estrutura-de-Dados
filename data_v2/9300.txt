tjioeng wanara perfected spelling wanara is a 1941 film from dutch east indies modern day indonesia directed and produced by jo eng seka starring r sukran elly joenara and ab djoenaedi it featured more than 500 people in supporting roles film follows a young prince named tjioeng wanara who must reclaim his throne from cruel king of galuh it is adapted from sundanese legend of same name second production by star film tjioeng wanara was released 18 august 1941 it was advertised heavily emphasising fact that scholar poerbatjaraka had served as historical adviser and that film was based on balai pustaka s version of legend it premiered to commercial success but received mixed reviews this black and white production which was screened until at least 1948 is now thought lost plot in 1255 many supporting actors tjioeng wanara was directed and produced by jo eng sek for star film it was second production by both jo and company following pah wongso pendekar boediman in 1941 poerbatjaraka starred r sukran elly joenara ab djoenaedi muhamad arief and s waldy waldy had made his film debut in 1940 s for oriental film company later joining star for pah wongso joenara and arief had begun their cinema careers in latter film tjioeng wanara featured first on screen performances by djoenaedi and sukran stars were supported by over 500 extras and actors in bit parts as such jb kristanto s katalog film indonesia records production as first colossal film in indies in terms of scale tjioeng wanara was based on a sundanese legend of same name retold by m a in a 1938 balai pustaka published
