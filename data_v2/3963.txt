michael joseph daly september 15 1924 july 25 2008 was an irish american united states army infantry officer who received united states military s highest decoration for valor medal of honor for his actions in world war ii he received medal for single handedly eliminating 15 german soldiers including a german patrol and destroying three machine gun nests he resigned from united states military academy after one year to fight in world war and was sent to europe participating in d day landings at omaha beach after d day invasion he fought on to germany where he was wounded he received a battlefield commission to second lieutenant returned to combat and participated in action that earned him medal of honor after being presented medal of honor in a ceremony at white house by president harry s truman he went back to his hometown started a family and became a businessman he died of cancer at his home in 2008 at age of 83 early life daly was born september 15 1924 in new york city but resided his entire life in fairfield connecticut except for one year he and his wife lived in county wicklow ireland his father colonel paul daly was a world war i and world war ii veteran who was a
