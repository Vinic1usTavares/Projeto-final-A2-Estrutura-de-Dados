alicia marie sacramone quinn born december 3 1987 is a retired american artistic gymnast she won a silver medal with united states team at 2008 summer olympics and is second most decorated american gymnast in world championship history with ten medals early years sacramone was born in boston on december 3 1987 to parents fred an and gail sacramone a hairstylist and salon owner she is of italian descent and has an older brother jonathan she graduated from winchester high school in 2006 she began studying dance at age of five and started gymnastics three years later in 1996 she trained with mihai and silvia brestyan at a club called gymnastics and more and followed them when they opened their own facility in ashland massachusetts brestyans served as sacramone s coaches for rest of her career sacramone began competing at elite level in 2002 and made national team for first time in 2003 elite career 2003 04 at 2003 national championships sacramone placed 14th in all around won a bronze medal on floor exercise and placed fourth on vault earning a spot on national team later that year she participated in her first international competition as a senior massilia gym cup in marseille france where she placed fourth on floor and ninth on vault in 2004 sacramone helped u s win a team gold medal at pacific alliance championships in honolulu and won individual vault title her performances caught attention of media which began to mention her as a contender for american team at 2004 olympics in athens however at 2004 u s nationals an error filled performance dashed her hopes of an olympic berth although she tied with mohini bhardwaj for silver medal on vault she finished in 19th place overall and did not qualify to olympic trials she also injured her back and required time off to recover sacramone continued competing in late 2004 as a member of national team and was assigned to several international meets including pan american individual event championships where she won vault and floor exercise titles at world cup finals in birmingham england she attracted media attention again when she upset reigning olympic vault champion monica of romania to take first place on event 2005 06 at 2005 national championships sacramone won individual titles on floor and vault scoring a 9 9 on floor and placed third on balance beam and fourth in all around she was named to american team along with nastia liukin and for 2005 world championships in melbourne australia where she won a gold medal on floor and placed third on vault she also defended her world cup vault title sacramone continued to compete for u s team in 2006 and participated in world championships in aarhus denmark where she won a silver medal with american squad and an individual silver on vault she defended her vault and floor titles at that year s u s nationals in september 2006 sacramone enrolled in brown university and joined school s gymnastics team during 2006 07 season she juggled a full ncaa competition schedule with her elite training at brestyan s she was first female american gymnast since kelly garrison in late 1980s to combine full time university studies and ncaa competition with elite gymnastics during her first year at brown she broke school s records for highest scores in all around vault and floor exercise she was named ecac rookie of year and swept ivy league classic becoming first gymnast to win all around and all four events and posting highest all around total ever recorded at competition she qualified as an individual on floor exercise for ncaa national championships second brown gymnast ever to do so but did not advance beyond preliminary round 2007 at 2007 national championships sacramone competed on three events choosing to skip uneven bars she defended her title on vault placed second on floor exercise and third on balance beam following nationals she was named to american team for world championships in stuttgart germany during preliminary round at world championships sacramone qualified for event finals on floor and vault and contributed to top qualifying position of american team she also posted a score on beam that would have been high enough to qualify her to beam final however due to an international federation of gymnastics rule allowing only two athletes per country to participate in each event final sacramone was unable to compete for a beam medal because teammates liukin and shawn johnson qualified ahead of her in team final she competed on vault beam and floor earning scores of 15 750 15 600 and 15 325 respectively when american team faltered after a difficult beam rotation in which two gymnasts made major errors sacramone gathered team for a pep talk to refocus them for floor american team earned 184 400 points overall which was good enough to secure gold medal ahead of china and romania in event finals sacramone received a bronze medal for her vault performance and a silver on floor behind johnson she was visibly upset by her performances and fought back tears after floor and during flower and medal ceremonies both brestyans and u s national team coordinator marta karolyi suggested that
