during organization s formative years of significant growth when cub scouting and exploring programs were established he developed bsa s national training center in early 1930s and was responsible for men s work for national council of churches in new york city and active in church work goodman is best remembered today for having created order of arrow oa a popular and highly successful program of bsa that continues to honor scouts for their cheerful service since its founding in 1915 order of arrow has grown to become a nationwide program having thousands of members which recognizes those
