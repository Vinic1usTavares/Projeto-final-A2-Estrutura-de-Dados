new york state route 128 ny 128 is a long north south state highway in northern westchester county new york route begins at an intersection with ny 22 just west of i 684 in hamlet of armonk crossing through downtown armonk route connects to ny 117 in southern end of village of mount kisco ny 128 was first constructed during early 1900s with a pair of contracts handed out by state of new york which constructed new alignment between ny 22 and triangle junction with ny 117 in mount kisco these segments were both added into state highway system in 1903 ny 128 itself was designated in 1930 renumbering of state highways from armonk to mount kisco this was extended in mid 1930s to connect to connecticut state line at road however in 1971 state truncated ny 128 back to ny 22 in armonk and
