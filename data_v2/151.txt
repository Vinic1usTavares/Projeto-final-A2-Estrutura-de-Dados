reginald walter reg saunders mbe 7 august 1920 2 march 1990 was first aboriginal australian to be commissioned as an officer in australian army he came from a military family his forebears having served in boer war and first world war enlisting as a soldier in 1940 he saw action during second world war in north africa greece and crete before being commissioned as a lieutenant and serving as a platoon commander in new guinea during 1944 1945 his younger brother harry also joined army and was killed in 1942 after war saunders was demobilised and returned to civilian life he later served as a company commander with 3rd battalion royal australian regiment 3 rar during korean war where he fought at battle of kapyong saunders left army in 1954 and worked in logging and metal industries before joining office of aboriginal affairs later department of aboriginal affairs as a liaison officer in 1969 in 1971 he was appointed a member of order of british empire mbe for his community service he died in 1990 aged 69 early life saunders was born near on framlingham aboriginal reserve in western victoria on 7 august 1920 he was a member of gunditjmara people o connell 2011 p 26 his father chris
