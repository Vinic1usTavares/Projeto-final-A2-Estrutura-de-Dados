joseph is an antiguan politician and chairman of antigua labour party alp first entering politics in 1984 when he was made a minister without portfolio in government of vere bird joseph became minister of finance seven years later renegotiating antiguan national debt and introducing fiscal reforms after a 1996 scandal in which it was discovered he had used his position to import a 1930s rolls royce for a friend bypassing normal import duties and taxes he was dismissed from bird administration returning 14 months later to serve as minister for planning implementation and environment following 1999 general election he became minister of heath and social improvement before being made minister of tourism and environment a few months later as minister joseph attempted to improve perception of antigua as a tourist destination and invest in industry spending 2 million us dollars increasing number of hotel rooms on island and providing money for both air jamaica and air luxor to provide flights to island outside of his ministerial duties he also met with jiang zhenghua vice chairman of standing committee of national people s congress having led official antiguan delegation to beijing following a power struggle in 2003 joseph was given economic development and investment promotion portfolio to hold at same time as his tourism and environment position despite a scandal surrounding donations he had received from texan businessman robert allen stanford joseph was selected to contest seat of st mary s north in 2004 antiguan general election in which alp lost power being replaced in government by united progressive party upp led by baldwin spencer joseph himself lost his seat but was elected chairman of alp a year later and regained seat in 2009 with a majority of 21 same year he was arrested for participating in an unauthorised may day rally protesting upp administration after a lengthy trial he and several other alp members were convicted and fined early career joseph entered cabinet level politics on 19 april 1984 when following alp s general election victory he was made a minister without portfolio in 1991 he became minister of finance and trade and along with financial secretary keith hurst and minister of economic development rodney williams negotiated rescheduling of antigua s national debt with european creditors government s 1992 budget announced and drafted by joseph included no new taxes but did involve a cut to government spending in response to what he claimed was reduced spending by tourists and smaller amounts of international aid in 1990 and 1991 1993 budget contained similar provisions ruling out possibility of an income tax as suggested by one of antigua s major trade unions this was accompanied by a programme of fiscal reforms including expenditure control improved revenue collection external debt management proper evaluation of projects improved public sector efficiency price controls on basic foods and commodities tax reform and good governance within parliament joseph campaigned for business licensing bill a statute which aimed to impose licensing fees ranging from 40 to 20 000 east caribbean dollars on those seeking to start businesses in certain fields bill attracted large amounts of controversy with critics such as hugh marshall asserting that it violated single market and economy agreement and general agreement on tariffs and trade and would lead to antiguan traders finding it difficult to expand into external markets although other ministers such as williams spoke in support of bill it was later shelved indefinitely with joseph saying that he found myself a lonely man dismissal in 1996 joseph left his position as finance minister after it was discovered that he had helped a friend import a 1930s rolls royce using his position to bypass normal import duties and taxes although government s immediate reaction was to deny that this had happened solicitor general issued a report in which he found that landing of rolls royce car was in violation of customs and port procedures facilitated by joseph s intervention with growing pressure joseph resigned on 22 september now on backbenches joseph became senior parliamentarian in charge of refugees and as such was government s chief spokesperson when eruption of a volcano in montserrat led to 3 000 refugees fleeing country and coming to antigua and barbuda reappointment joseph s position on backbenches did not last long on 3 december 1997 less than two years after his resignation he was recalled to take up planning implementation and environment portfolio with bird saying that his managerial skills are required at this juncture of our economic history when several projects require implementation to get off ground quickly following 1999 general election he was again appointed to cabinet this time as minister of heath and social improvement with planning portfolio going to benjamin a few months later he became minister of tourism and environment with bernard percival taking over his health portfolio as minister joseph oversaw a back to basics approach to tourism attempting to have nation perceived as one known for its cleanliness natural beauty and pristine environment as part of this antiguan government invested 2 million us dollars in boosting number of hotel rooms available from 3 000 to 5 000 and opened antigua hotel institute seeking to train in how to more efficiently and effectively work in tourism industry joseph also oversaw government buying a third of jolly beach resort in an attempt to save it from bankruptcy establishing a management company to oversee property in february 2000 he led government delegation in an official visit to beijing where they met with jiang zhenghua vice chairman of standing committee of national people s congress to discuss bilateral relations and issues of common concern he also led antiguan delegation to world travel market in 2001 following joseph s promise of government investiture in route air jamaica resumed flights to antigua in 2002 having ceased flying there six years earlier this was followed by announcement that air luxor would similarly begin offering flights to islands after a financial scandal in early 2003 alp backbencher bowen asked for a vote of no confidence in bird s government on 19 may calling for him to step down and accusing him resign if michael was not immediately removed within a few hours michael s resignation had been formally tendered soon afterwards joseph was given economic development and investment promotion portfolio to hold at same time as his tourism and environment position later career in late 2003 it was discovered that texan businessman allen stanford had given both joseph and fellow minister gaston browne cheques worth 100 000 east caribbean dollars while browne denied suggestions that this had constituted bribery claiming that money had gone towards community projects leader of opposition baldwin spencer demanded that both ministers resign both ministers were part of a team negotiating with stanford over a land exchange in st john s and news led to protesters picketing their joseph and browne antigua had a general election in 2004 following dissolution of parliament in late february with alp running a full slate joseph was nominated for st mary s north seat alp were
