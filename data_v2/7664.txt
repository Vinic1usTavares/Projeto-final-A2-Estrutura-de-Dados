jamila and president indonesian jamila dan sang presiden is a 2009 indonesian film directed by ratna sarumpaet and starring atiqah hasiholan and christine hakim it follows story of a prostitute sentenced to death for killing a government minister film was adapted from a stage play entitled pelacur dan sang presiden prostitute and president which sarumpaet had written after receiving a grant from unicef to study child trafficking trends in indonesia and promote awareness of problem in preparing script she spent several months interviewing prostitutes in several cities film itself spent three years in production and featured much of same cast and crew as original play released on 30 april 2009 film received mixed to positive reception in indonesia internationally film featured in several film festivals and won awards in france italy and taiwan it was submitted to 82nd academy awards for best foreign language film but not nominated plot jamila and president opens with narration from jamila atiqah hasiholan a victim of human trafficking followed by several scenes showing her living a glamorous yet unfulfilling night life after hearing a news report that a government minister nurdin has been murdered jamila surrenders herself to police this surprises her would be boyfriend ibrahim dwi who feels badly for jamila and begins working towards freeing her under orders of president jamila is placed in a prison outside jakarta where she receives rough treatment at hands of guards and warden ria christine hakim in prison ria reads jamila s diary and learns her backstory jamila was sold by her mother to a middleman who then sold her to a rich family while living with family jamila is raped by both father and son in succession jamila kills son and runs away while mother c noer aware of their actions kills her husband jamila becomes a worker at a market but once again must escape when she learns that some local men plan to rape her she escapes to a discotheque and when it is raided by police she is thought to be a prostitute and arrested after being released jamila is raised by a kind hearted prostitute named susi ria irawan who was also caught in raid in present day several groups are demanding that jamila be given death sentence one male guard surya takes pity on jamila and tries to help her however jamila ignores him ria although slowly becoming more sympathetic to jamila gets into an argument with her over nurdin s murder this results in jamila being placed in isolation several days later court finds jamila guilty of murder and sentences her to death a sentence which will be carried out in 36 hours ria visits jamila in her cell to check on her and ria explains that she intends to ask president for a stay jamila refuses then tells ria about her experience looking for her sister fatimah in borneo how she killed man who had put fatimah in a brothel but was unable to find her sister day before jamila s execution ibrahim meets with susi who tells him about jamila s romantic involvement with nurdin jamila became pregnant with nurdin s child and insisted that he take responsibility but instead nurdin told her he was marrying someone else and humiliated her in public when two met in a hotel nurdin threatened jamila with a pistol in self defence jamila killed him with pistol in modern day jamila walks towards her execution president has not responded to request for stay screen fades to black as a gunshot goes off and afterwards statistics about child trafficking and prostitution are shown production jamila and president was directed by ratna sarumpaet a women s rights activist film sarumpaet s directorial debut was based on a theatrical drama she had previously made entitled pelacur dan sang presiden prostitute and president which was put on in 2006 in five cities by satu merah panggung theatre troup she was inspired write story in 2005 after unicef approached her to do a survey of child trafficking in southeast asia and promote awareness of problem while writing original script sarumpaet interviewed prostitutes in surabaya surakarta garut and borneo over a period of six months this research carried over into film sarumpaet cast her daughter atiqah hasiholan in lead role initially hasiholan viewed her character as a regular slutty prostitute but after thinking of character more thoroughly she considered jamila a victim of life to better play her character hasiholan visited several prostitution districts and spoke with sex workers there learning their motivations she drew conclusion that poverty was a deciding factor senior actress christine hakim a close friend of sarumpaet s was cast as ria it was her first antagonistic role sarumpaet s brother sam was assistant director and helped calm his sister when technical glitches occurred much of cast and crew including star hasiholan had previously acted in stage play sarumpaet reported that film cost rp 6 5 billion us 800 000 to produce she originally had difficulty finding sponsors film spent three years in production themes sarumpaet described film as being about how awful effect of poverty on human s morality and lives is in an interview with tempo magazine she stated that she did not have a political message but intended to just show facts yazid in a review for jakarta post wrote that film was of women who suffer continuously genre common in indonesian cinema he compared theme to ponirah ponirah is convicted 1984 starring hakim and slamet rahardjo s also writing for jakarta post described film as depicting grim reality of those whose existence viewers are rarely aware of victims of child and women trafficking style majority of film takes place inside prison a review for jakarta globe notes that film has a spartan feel as supported by its cinematography it uses periods of silence to evoke an emotional response from viewers with flashbacks used to drive story president is never shown instead he is represented by long shots of merdeka palace a presidential residence in jakarta release and reception jamila and president was originally intended to be released on 10 december 2007 in commemoration of human rights day however due to production difficulties it was delayed for almost two years film premiered at fx plaza in jakarta on 27 april 2009 with a wide release three days later it received mixed reception yazid wrote that film was easily one of most noteworthy indonesian films in recent years and praised hakim s acting however he found hasiholan s acting overly theatrical a review for jakarta globe described film as engaging because of its dramatic subject and well measured pace but ultimately leaving audience with unanswered questions due to number of characters introduced marcel thee and armando siahaan writing in same newspaper in december 2009 selected jamila and president as best indonesian film of 2009 noting that titular character s struggle provides an emotional roller coaster ride writing for tempo stated film came across as without a climax with hasiholan s acting at times overly theatrical and hakim s coming across flat eko sofyan reviewing for kompas wrote that film reminded viewers that poverty and prostitution were still major issues and had to be dealt with before indonesia could further develop in indonesia jamila and president was nominated for several awards at 2009 indonesian film festival film received six nominations for best picture best director best adapted screenplay best editing best sound arrangement and best artistic arrangement it did not win any internationally jamila and president was screened at several film festivals including in bangkok hong kong and australia at asiatica film mediale in rome it won a netpac award it later won an award for best editing at 53rd asia pacific film festival in taipei taiwan jamila and president was chosen by indonesian filmmakers and television association to represent indonesia at 82nd academy awards for best foreign language film however film was not nominated at vesoul international film festival of asian cinema in vesoul france jamila and president won two awards prix de public and prix
