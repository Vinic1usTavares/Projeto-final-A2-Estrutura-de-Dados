an escort for convoys to europe on 17th she departed new york with convoy hx 49 a group of eighteen ships rest of escort included destroyer and armed merchant cruiser amc rochester and british amc at before war ended in november 1918 in december she was equipped to carry american soldiers back from france she made four such trips to brest france and carried some 4 540 men back to united states first trip began on 30 december she reached brest on 11 january 1919 and after embarking a contingent of soldiers departed for newport news virginia where they arrived on 28 january fourth trip on 2 july 1920 ship was decommissioned and on 15th she was reclassified as bb 14 she remained out of service until 12 july 1922 when she was stricken from naval register in accordance with washington
