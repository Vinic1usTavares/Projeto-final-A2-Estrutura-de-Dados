sorkjosen airport is a regional airport located at village of sorkjosen in nordreisa municipality in troms county norway about from municipal center of storslett owned and operated by state owned avinor it handled 15 198 passengers in 2014 airport has a runway and is served by wideroe which operates regional routes using dash 8 100 to tromso and communities in finnmark on public service obligation contracts airport opened in 1974 and was originally served using de havilland canada twin otter aircraft dash 8s were introduced in 1995 and two years later ownership was transferred from nordreisa municipality to state history sorkjosen was launched as part of a national network of regional short take off and landing airport which was proposed in mid 1960s final decision to build airport was taken by parliament in 1972 both wideroe and norving applied to operate subsidized regional routes in finnmark which included route to sorkjosen wideroe was awarded contract in 1973 sorkjosen airport opened on 1 august 1974 same day as four regional airports in finnmark thumb left terminal building a new fire station serving both airport and municipality was completed in 1992
