por debajo de la mesa underneath table is a song written by armando manzanero and performed by mexican recording artist luis miguel arranged by bebu silvetti it was one of two original compositions written for miguel s fifteenth studio album romances it was released as lead single from album on 15 july 1997 and it became his thirteenth number one single on billboard hot latin songs chart in united states music video features miguel performing at a fine dining restaurant in new york city track received a negative reaction from achy obejas of chicago tribune who called it lame it was nominated pop song of year at 10th annual lo nuestro awards and manzanero was awarded a broadcast music inc bmi latin award for writing song manzanero performed record as a duet with several artists such as tania libertad and susana zabaleta background in 1991 miguel released romance a collection of classic latin ballads oldest of which dates back to 1940s album was produced by armando manzanero and arranged by bebu silvetti and was credited for revitalizing bolero genre it also made history as first spanish language album to be certified gold by recording industry association of america riaa in united states a follow up to romance was released in 1994 under title segundo romance second romance which was produced by miguel juan carlos calderon and kiko cibrian both albums received a platinum certification by riaa in united states and also became successful in countries outside of latin america and united states such as finland and saudi arabia selling over twelve million copies combined in december 1996 miguel held a press conference in buenos aires argentina where he announced his desire to record a third romance album and mentioned possibility of working with manzanero and juan gabriel he also expressed an interest in singing in italian and portuguese although album s songs are originally all in spanish two months later manzanero confirmed that he was working with miguel on another bolero inspired ballads album under tentative title tercer romance third romance miguel s record label confirmed that fourteen tracks would be included on album under title romances por debajo de la mesa was written by manzanero as one of two original compositions for album along with contigo estar contigo manzanero stated that por debajo de la mesa and contigo were two of his favorite songs that he composed for miguel promotion a music video for por debajo de la mesa was filmed in new york city which features miguel performing at rainbow room with video being shot in black and white it was directed by daniela federici song was included on his greatest hits album mis boleros 2002 and grandes exitos 2005 a live version of song was included on his album vivo 2000 as part of romances medley release and reception por debajo de la mesa serviced to radio stations across latin america on 5 july in united states song debuted at number two on billboard hot latin songs chart on week of 2 august 1997 six weeks later it peaked at number one on chart where and remained on position for four weeks por debajo de la mesa ended 1997 as eighth best performing latin single of year in united states track also peaked at number one on billboard latin pop songs chart where it spent five weeks on top of chart and was best performing latin pop song of year in country despite giving
