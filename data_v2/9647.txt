george vi albert frederick arthur george 14 december 1895 6 february 1952 was king of united kingdom and dominions of british commonwealth from 11 december 1936 until his death on 6 february 1952 he became known as a symbol of british determination to win second world war against germany known as bertie among his family and close friends george vi was born in reign of his great grandmother queen victoria and was named after his great grandfather albert prince consort as second son of king george v he was not expected to inherit throne and spent his early life in shadow of his elder brother edward he attended naval college as a teenager and served in royal navy and royal air force during first world war in 1920 he was made duke of york he married lady elizabeth bowes lyon in 1923 and they had two daughters elizabeth and margaret in mid 1920s he had speech therapy for a stammer which he never fully overcame george s elder brother ascended throne as edward viii upon death of their father in 1936 however edward was forced to choose between crown and marriage to divorced american socialite wallis simpson with abdication of edward to marry simpson george ascended throne as third monarch of house of windsor from 1939 british empire and commonwealth except ireland declared war on nazi germany war with kingdom of italy and empire of japan followed in 1940 and 1941 respectively king and his family remained in london during blitz and his popularity soared as he shared hardships of common people britain and its allies were victorious in 1945 but british empire declined ireland had largely broken away followed by independence of india and pakistan in 1947 george relinquished title of emperor of india in june 1948 and instead adopted new title of head of commonwealth he was
