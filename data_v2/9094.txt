alexandra of denmark alexandra caroline marie charlotte louise julia 1 december 1844 20 november 1925 was queen consort of united kingdom and british dominions and empress consort of india as wife of king edward vii alexandra s family had been relatively obscure until 1852 when her father prince christian of schleswig holstein sonderburg glucksburg was chosen with consent of major european powers to succeed his distant cousin frederick vii of denmark at age of sixteen she was chosen as future wife of albert edward prince of wales heir apparent of queen victoria they married eighteen months later in 1863 same year her father became king christian ix of denmark and her brother was appointed as king george i of greece she was princess of wales from 1863 to 1901 longest anyone has ever held that title and became generally popular her style of dress and bearing were copied by fashion conscious women largely excluded from wielding any political power she unsuccessfully attempted to sway opinion of british ministers and her husband s family to favour greek and danish interests her public duties were restricted to uncontroversial involvement in charitable work on death of queen
