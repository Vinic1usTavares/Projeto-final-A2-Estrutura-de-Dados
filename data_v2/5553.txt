collaborator luis fernando ochoa and belongs to very rock and roll direction of album musically sale el sol is a folk and latin music influenced alternative rock track its lyrics encourage one to be optimistic during difficult times epic records released sale el sol as second single from album on 4 january 2011 critical reception towards song was positive with many critics praising its
