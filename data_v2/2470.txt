related to panigrahana ritual swami parmeshwaranand p 66 is iconographical depiction of wedding of hindu deities shiva and parvati couple are often depicted performing panigrahana accepting hand ritual of a hindu wedding where groom accepts bride by taking her right hand in his couple depicted in centre are accompanied by a host of divinities and other celestial beings god vishnu and his wife divine marriage in annual temple festivals however kalyanasundara scenes are found across india in caves sculptures and on temple walls legend various hindu scriptures narrate story of union of shiva
