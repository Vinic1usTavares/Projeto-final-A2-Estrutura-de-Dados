electra heart is second studio album by welsh singer marina diamandis released under stage name marina and diamonds it was released on 27 april 2012 by 679 artists and atlantic records diamandis collaborated with producers including liam howe greg kurstin dr luke diplo and stargate during its recording and subsequently transitioned from new wave musical styles seen throughout her debut studio album family jewels 2010 their efforts resulted in a concept album consisting of electropop and dance pop music a distinct departure from her earlier projects lyrically album discusses topics of love and identity diamandis created title character electra heart to represent female archetypes in popular american culture house wife beauty queen homewrecker and idle teen music critics were divided in their opinions of electra heart expressing ambivalence towards diamandis shift in musical style and its overall production album debuted at number one on uk albums chart with first week sales of 21 358 copies in doing so it earned diamandis her first chart topping album there although it later became lowest selling number one album of 21st century in country album was eventually certified gold by british phonographic industry bpi for exceeding shipments of 100 000 units electra heart performed moderately on international charts including a peak position of number 31 on us billboard 200 and became diamandis highest charting album in united states at time electra heart was supported by three singles all of which were supplemented by music videos primadonna was released as lead single from album on 20 march 2012 and peaked at number 11 on uk singles chart follow up singles power control and how to be a heartbreaker were respectively released on 20 july and 7 december and reached numbers 193 and 88 in united kingdom promotional single radioactive charted inside top 40 in several countries album was additionally promoted by diamandis headlining lonely hearts club tour which visited europe and north america from may 2012 through may 2013 background and production after returning from united states after launch of her debut studio album family jewels 2010 diamandis considered creating a character which would become centerpiece of her follow up project she commented that she was inspired by tumblr generation to photograph herself in several places across united states announced electra heart in august 2011 it was initially planned to become a three piece project inspired by american culture in 1970s although it eventually evolved into her second studio album production and approximately 22 songs were recorded for potential inclusion on album she later commented that record was dedicated to dysfunctional love elaborating that rejection is a universally
