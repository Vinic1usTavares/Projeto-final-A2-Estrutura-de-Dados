hurricane henri was a rare tropical cyclone that entered gulf of mexico without having made landfall it was second of four times this occurred during 20th century eighth named storm and fifth hurricane of 1979 atlantic hurricane season it formed on september 14 in northwestern caribbean sea throughout much of its duration henri moved erratically and initially maintained a general westward track on september 16 it attained tropical storm status and a day later it reached hurricane status by two days later after experiencing hostile conditions henri weakened to tropical depression status as it turned to northeast and on september 24 it merged with a frontal low in northeast gulf of mexico due to its slow and erratic motion hurricane forced evacuations along mexican coastline its remnants brought rainfall and flooding to florida panhandle meteorological history hurricane henri developed as tropical depression eighteen in extreme northwestern caribbean sea on september 14 from a tropical wave which had previously moved off coast of africa formation of a tropical depression was confirmed by reports from hurricane hunters depression tracked northward brushing eastern portion henri on september 16 tropical storm henri continued to intensify as it tracked through bay of campeche on september 17 storm turned northwestward after ridge to its north weakened and later that day henri reached hurricane status six hours later it reached peak winds of 85 mph 140 km h about 150 miles 245 km northeast of veracruz subsequently a broad low pressure area developed over western gulf of mexico causing motion of henri to become erratic on september 18 cyclone began a steady weakening trend believed to have been caused by land interaction and funneling of moisture toward a developing disturbance near texas coast henri turned eastward on september 19 and weakened to tropical depression status it failed to regain significant convection and it turned northeastward along an extended cold front on september 24 henri merged with frontal trough in northeastern gulf of mexico hurricane henri was one of only four hurricanes to enter gulf of mexico without making landfall
