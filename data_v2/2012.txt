2 14th battalion was an infantry battalion of australian army that served during world war ii part of 21st brigade 7th division battalion was raised from second australian imperial force volunteers drawn mainly from state of victoria after completing training in australia in 1940 battalion deployed to middle east where it was stationed in egypt and palestine before it saw action against vichy french in syria in june and july 1941 in a short lived campaign garrison duties in lebanon followed before battalion was withdrawn to australia in early 1942 as australian forces were concentrated in pacific to respond to threat posed by japan s entry into war after a short period of re training in australia to prepare for jungle warfare battalion was deployed to new guinea in august 1942 as australians sent reinforcements to kokoda track to fight against japanese forces that had been advancing towards port moresby after japanese were forced to exhaust their supplies they began to fall back towards their beachheads on north coast 2 14th was part of australian advance that then saw further action around gona in september 1943 after a period of re organisation in australia battalion took part in advance on lae as allies went on offensive in new guinea before taking part in fighting in markham and ramu valleys of finisterre range campaign battalion s final involvement in war came in landing on balikpapan in 1945 2 14th was disbanded after war in early 1946 history formation 2 14th battalion was raised on 26 april 1940 at puckapunyal victoria shortly after start of world war ii
