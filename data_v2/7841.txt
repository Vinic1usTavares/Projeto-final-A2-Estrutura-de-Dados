theatre europe is a turn based strategy video game developed and published by personal software services it was first released in united kingdom for commodore 64 zx spectrum amstrad cpc and atari 8 bit home computers in 1985 it was later released in france by ere informatique in 1986 and was released in united states by later that year it was also ported to tatung einstein home computer in 1989 exclusively in united kingdom it is fifth instalment of strategic wargames series game is set during a fictional war in europe between nato and warsaw pact in which both sides use nuclear and chemical weapons against each other main objective of game is to fight conventional battles in continental europe whilst trying to avoid a worldwide nuclear holocaust throughout game various capital cities and their civilian populations will be destroyed by nuclear weapons game will only end once either side is forced to surrender or if entire population of europe perishes in order to request a nuclear strike player was required to call a dedicated telephone number which led to an automated message announcing authorisation code during development developers obtained extensive information and statistics of military strength from ministry of defence and soviet embassy in london theatre europe gained national controversy upon release receiving criticism from both campaign for nuclear
