long construction time was in part due to a redesign that was completed after work on ship had begun her main battery of four guns was initially to be placed in a central armored battery but during redesign this was altered to a pair of twin gun turrets amidships grosser kurfurst was sunk on her maiden voyage in an accidental collision with ironclad two ships along with were steaming in english channel on 31 may 1878 three ships encountered a group of fishing boats and in turning to avoid them grosser kurfurst inadvertently crossed too closely to konig wilhelm latter rammed grosser kurfurst which sank in span of about eight minutes taking between 269 and 276 of her crew with her her loss spurred a series of investigations into circumstances of collision which ultimately resulted in acquittal of both rear admiral squadron commander and count alexander von monts captain of grosser kurfurst political infighting over affair led to ousting of rear admiral reinhold von werner from
