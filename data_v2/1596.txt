attack squadrons flying cac wirraways douglas bostons and bristol beaufighters with which it took part in new guinea campaign until mid year it was then reorganised with three fighter squadrons operating p 40 kittyhawks and supermarine spitfires in this form it saw action in new britain and admiralty islands campaigns through 1943 44 wing was disbanded at los negros in august 1944 and by
