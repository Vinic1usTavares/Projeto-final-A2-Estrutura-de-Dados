friedrich hans ulrich muhe 20 june 1953 22 july 2007 was a german film television and theatre actor he played role of hauptmann captain gerd wiesler in oscar winning film das leben der anderen lives of others 2006 for which he received gold award for best performance by an actor in a leading role at deutscher filmpreis german film awards and best actor award at 2006 european film awards after leaving school muhe was employed as a construction worker and a border guard at berlin wall he then turned to acting and from late 1970s into 1980s appeared in numerous plays becoming a star of deutsches theater in east berlin he was active in politics and denounced communist rule in east germany in a memorable address at alexanderplatz demonstration on 4 november 1989 shortly before fall of berlin wall after german reunification he continued to appear in a large number of films television programmes and theatre productions in germany he was particularly known for playing lead role of dr robert kolmaar in long running forensic crime series der letzte zeuge last witness 1998 2007 early life and education thumb volksbuhne in rosa luxemburg platz in borough of mitte berlin photographed
