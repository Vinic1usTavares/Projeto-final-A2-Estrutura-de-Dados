hurricane philippe was a short lived hurricane that formed over atlantic in september during 2005 atlantic hurricane season philippe was sixteenth named storm and ninth hurricane of season hurricane philippe initially formed to east of lesser antilles on september 17 and moved to north strengthening as it did so philippe became a hurricane on september 18 and stayed as such for two days before increasing wind shear from a non tropical system took its toll on september 20 and weakened philippe into a tropical storm philippe continued to weaken as it looped around this low and was absorbed by it on september 23 meteorological history on september 9 a tropical wave moved off african coast and moved west into atlantic it started to become more organized on september 13 and national hurricane center began to watch it closely for further development tropical depression seventeen formed from wave on september 17 when it was 350 miles 560 km east of barbados depression strengthened further to become tropical storm philippe that evening and official forecasts correctly indicated that philippe would move northwards and not approach lesser antilles despite climatology suggesting an impact on islands philippe continued to steadily strengthen becoming a hurricane late on september 18 as it traveled north in a low shear environment and some models suggested that this trend would continue and philippe would go on to become a major hurricane however philippe began to encounter higher wind shear from a non tropical low development of which partly resulted from hurricane rita s outflow as a result of this shear philippe s winds only managed to reach a peak of 80 mph 130 km h before weakening back into a tropical storm on september 20 philippe moved farther north skirting low and continued to weaken gradually official forecast did not predict this primarily as a result of some models indicating a reintensification to hurricane status philippe began to loop around center of non tropical system weakening as it did so as philippe turned towards bermuda it weakened into a tropical depression on september 22 depression degenerated into a remnant low next day and this remnant continued to loop cyclonically and could be tracked for a further day or two within circulation of non tropical system preparations impact and naming despite forming fairly close to lesser antilles no tropical cyclone warnings were issued for islands as official forecast correctly foresaw that philippe would stay well to east a tropical storm warning was issued for bermuda on september 23 but proved redundant when philippe dissipated well to south of island storm still brought gusty winds and moisture to island with 0 15 inches 3 8 mm of precipitation reported on september 23 circulation that absorbed philippe dropped light rainfall on island and was responsible for lowest barometric pressure during month when tropical storm philippe formed on september 17 it was earliest ever in
