new york state route 270 ny 270 is a north south state highway in western new york in united states it runs through rural and wooded areas of town of amherst in erie county and town of pendleton in niagara county southern terminus of route is at an intersection with ny 263 south of hamlet of getzville its northern terminus is at a junction with ny 31 and ny 93 west of city of lockport entirety of ny 270 is known as campbell boulevard ny 270 was assigned as part of 1930 renumbering of state highways in new york route once extended as far south as ny 5 and as far north as ny 104 however it
