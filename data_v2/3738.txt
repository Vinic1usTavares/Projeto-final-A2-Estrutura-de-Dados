associated with italian saint francis of assisi 1226 but entirely absent from his writings prayer in its present form has not been traced back further than 1912 its first known occurrence was in french in a small spiritual magazine called la clochette little bell published by a catholic church organization in paris named la ligue de la sainte messe league of holy mass author s name was not songwriters and quoted by prominent leaders and its broadly inclusive language has found appeal with diverse faiths encouraging service to others text in most of published versions of prayer text is
