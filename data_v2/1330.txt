these days live in concert is a live album by australian alternative rock band powderfinger released as a cd on 6 september 2004 and as a two disc dvd on 4 october 2004 album consists of songs performed by powderfinger in concerts at sydney entertainment centre and fox studios in sydney most of songs performed are from powderfinger s prior studio album vulture street these days live in concert earned a mixed response from reviewers some enjoyed energy and flair of live performances while others disliked lack of dynamism production these days live in concert disc one was directed by gregor jordan who band had first encountered when he requested they write these days for his film two hands bernard fanning also worked with jordan on film ned kelly in 2003 band chose jordan because they did not want to present these days live in concert as just straight up live concert footage but instead wanted to present it from a different angle jordan agreed and believed that band being a successful rock group from brisbane was an interesting angle to work from disc one of these days live in concert was recorded by powderfinger during sellout performances at sydney entertainment centre and featured live performances of 14 songs including nine singles footage on disc was shot over two nights disc two low key was directed by bernie zelvis of channel v and was recorded at sydney s fox studios disc contained mostly acoustic performances by band and included a cover of inner city blues by rodriguez band s live performances at large venues such as sydney entertainment centre was criticised by some fans as band had previously stated they disliked playing to large crowds drummer jon coghill said he was not bothered by comments as band other than guitarist ian haug generally did not take much note of such critique release and response these days live in concert was released as a cd on 6 september 2004 through record label universal music australia only 40 000 copies of cd were initially created meanwhile dvd version was released on 4 october 2004 stumblin first and only single from album was released on 6 june 2004 but failed to chart these days live in concert entered aria charts at 2 19 september 2004 and spent a total of 8 weeks in chart exiting on 7 november 2004 cd version was certified gold by aria while dvd version was certified double platinum in same year these days live in concert received a mixed response from reviewers sputnikmusic reviewer james bishop approved of album giving it a score of 4 5 and summarising powderfinger deliver a live show that s as entertaining as it is surprising bishop praised numerous performances on disc one calling rockin rocks a great introduction to their arena spectacular describing almost instrument free performance of these days as one of most heart wrenching moments they ve ever recorded and calling thrilloilogy quintessential live powderfinger track bishop also enjoyed disc two lauding acoustic performances of sunsets and love your way and describing b side rita as an absolute hit as usual jeff crawford of adelaide newspaper messenger disagreed with bishop giving album three stars and stating gregor jordan s concert film makes most of powderfinger s strengths but can t disguise their lack of dynamism while interviews are hardly insightful he called fanning s solo on these days
