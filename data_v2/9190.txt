skaters in bois de boulogne is an oil on canvas landscape painting by french artist pierre auguste renoir created during winter of 1868 painting depicts a snowscape with many parisians young and old spending leisure time on a frozen park lake due to renoir s strong dislike of cold temperatures and snow piece is one of his few winter landscapes background pierre auguste renoir 1841 1919 first met
