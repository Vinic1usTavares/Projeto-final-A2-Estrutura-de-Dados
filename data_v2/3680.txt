velaiilla pattadhari popularly referred to as vip is a 2014 indian tamil language action comedy film written and filmed by velraj in his directorial debut film features dhanush amala paul in lead roles while vivek saranya ponvannan samuthirakani and surbhi play supporting roles dhanush besides acting producing and distributing venture in association with escape artists motion pictures also worked as lyricist and album producer on film s original soundtrack film s album and score were composed by anirudh ravichander film focuses on raghuvaran a graduate who is unemployed for four years and how he gets a job whilst successfully overcoming various obstacles before and after finding employment principal photography commenced in august 2013 and was completed by may 2014 with film being shot primarily in chennai film was released on 18 july 2014 and received positive critical reception film grossed 190 8 million on its opening weekend and earned a worldwide collection 530 million during its lifetime run film was dubbed into telugu as raghuvaran b tech which was released on 1 january 2015 it was remade in kannada as brihaspathi 2018 a sequel to film directed by soundarya rajinikanth was released on 11 august 2017 plot raghuvaran is a graduate in civil engineering but has been unemployed for four years he is adamant about working in a job related to his field only and is unwilling to accept any other profession he often comes into conflict with his father who considers him irresponsible his younger brother karthik works in an it company earning well and evidently his father s approval raghuvaran s mother bhuvana however supports him raghuvaran falls for his neighbour s daughter shalini who hates him initially but warms up to him after hearing about
