eastern terminus is at a junction with ny 64 and ny 96 in village of pittsford ny 252 passes through center of town of henrietta s commercial district where it intersects ny 15 route passes through three distinct areas a lightly populated rural area of chili west of genesee river heavily developed commercial district centered on ny 252 s intersections with ny 15 and ny 15a and a mostly
