city of angels is a song by american rock band thirty seconds to mars featured on their fourth studio album love lust faith and dreams 2013 written by lead vocalist jared leto who co produced song with steve lillywhite city of angels was inspired by leto s experience of living in los angeles with his family and was influenced by city s culture imbued with elements of synthrock as well as music from 1980s track was cited as an example of album s variety and experimentation it was one of first songs to be written for love lust faith and dreams but required a long period of time to record city of angels was released as a promotional single in july 2013 in united states and was serviced to mainstream radio in europe in october 2013 it received general acclaim from music critics who commended song s composition and production following release of love lust faith and dreams track appeared in lower regions of uk rock chart when released as a single it re entered chart reaching number 21 peaked at number eight on alternative songs in us and experienced moderate success in some international markets due to digital sales from album a piano version of song was digitally released in july 2014 jared leto directed music video for song which features several personalities joining three members of thirty seconds to mars in sharing their visions about los angeles video was favorably reviewed by critics who complimented simplicity and cohesion with song s message it received loudwire music award for best rock video and was nominated for best cinematography at 2014 mtv video music
