carlson s patrol also known as long patrol or carlson s long patrol was an operation by 2nd marine raider battalion under command of evans carlson during guadalcanal campaign against imperial japanese army from 6 november to 4 december 1942 in operation 2nd raiders attacked forces under command of toshinari shoji which were escaping from an attempted encirclement in koli point area on guadalcanal and attempting to rejoin other japanese army units on opposite side of u s lunga perimeter in a series of small unit engagements over 29 days 2nd raiders killed almost 500 japanese soldiers while suffering only 16 killed although many were afflicted by disease raiders also captured a japanese field gun that was delivering harassing fire on henderson field allied airfield at lunga point on
