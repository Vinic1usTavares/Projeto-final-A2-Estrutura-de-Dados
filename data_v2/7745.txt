allan macinnis born july 11 1963 is a canadian former professional ice hockey defenceman who played 23 seasons in national hockey league nhl for calgary flames and st louis blues a first round selection of flames in 1981 nhl entry draft he went on to become a 12 time all star he was named conn smythe trophy winner as most valuable player of playoffs in 1989 after leading flames to stanley cup championship he was voted winner of james norris memorial trophy in 1999 as top defenceman in league while a member of blues in 2017 macinnis was named one of 100 greatest nhl players in history macinnis was most famous for having hardest shot in league he tied bobby orr s ontario hockey league ohl record for goals by a defenceman and won two ohl championships and a memorial cup with kitchener rangers as a junior he famously split goaltender mike liut s mask with a shot and became only fourth defenceman in nhl history to score 100 points in a season internationally he was an all star on defence as canada won 1991 canada cup and twice participated in winter olympics he was a member of 2002 team that won canada s first gold medal in 50 years an eye injury suffered early in 2003 04 nhl season forced macinnis into retirement he finished his career third all time among defencemen in goals assists and points and was named to seven post season all star teams he was elected to
