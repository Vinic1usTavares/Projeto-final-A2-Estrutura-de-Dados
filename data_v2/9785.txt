lev chernyi died september 21 1921 was a russian individualist anarchist theorist activist and poet and a leading figure of third russian revolution in 1917 chernyi was released from his political imprisonment by imperial russian regime and swiftly became one of leading figures in russian anarchism after strongly denouncing new bolshevik government in various anarchist publications and joining several underground resistance movements chernyi was arrested by cheka on a charge of counterfeiting and in 1921 was executed without trial early life philosophy and imprisonment chernyi was born pavel to an army colonel father a declasse intellectual whom anarchist historian paul avrich compares with volin chernyi advocated a nietzschean overthrow of values of bourgeois russian society and rejected voluntary communes of anarcho communist peter kropotkin as a threat to freedom of individual chernyi advocated free association of independent individuals in a book titled associational anarchism and published in 1907 scholars including avrich and allan have interpreted this vision of society to have been greatly influenced by individualist anarchists max stirner and benjamin tucker subsequent to book s publication chernyi was imprisoned in siberia under russian czarist regime for his revolutionary activities return to moscow and opposition to bolsheviks on his return from siberia in 1917 chernyi enjoyed great popularity among moscow workers as a lecturer and was at this time one of russia s leading individualist anarchists and one of anarchism s main ideologues he was secretary and leading theorist of moscow federation of anarchist groups which was formed in march 1917 after abdication of tsar nicholas ii and was primarily concerned with disseminating propaganda to moscow s poorer classes a personal acquaintance of lev and other leading bolsheviks chernyi denounced nascent russian soviet republic at a rally on march 5 1918 declaring that for anarchists socialist state was as much an enemy as its bourgeois predecessor and promising to paralyze governmental mechanism a vociferous advocate of seizing private homes chernyi agitated against state in pages of anarchist weekly newspaper proposing increasingly detailed means of decentralized production and complete absence of internal power structures in spring of 1918 anarchist groups within moscow federation formed armed detachments in reaction to growing repression of all resistance and free expression these were black guards precursors to anarchist black army which fought bolsheviks in russian civil war on night of april 11 cheka soviet secret police raided a building occupied by moscow federation with official aim of arresting and charging robber bands in anarchist ranks they were met with armed resistance by black guards and in ensuing battle approximately forty anarchists were killed or wounded and about five hundred were imprisoned arrest and execution thumb eminent bolshevik nikolai bukharin who was wounded in anarchist attack on moscow communist party headquarters having helped establish an underground group in 1918 chernyi joined another group called underground anarchists following year organization which had been founded by kazimir and piotr published two issues of an incendiary broadsheet denouncing communist dictatorship as worst tyranny in human history on september 25 1919 together with a number of leftist social revolutionaries underground anarchists bombed headquarters of moscow committee of communist party during a plenary meeting twelve communists were killed and fifty five others were wounded including eminent bolshevik theorist and pravda editor nikolai bukharin chernyi was detained along with baron on a counterfeiting charge in august 1921 moscow izvestia published an official report announcing that ten anarchist bandits among them chernyi had been shot without hearing or trial however historian of anarchism paul avrich contends that chernyi was executed in september of that year rather than august although he was not personally involved in bombing of communist party headquarters chernyi was because of his association with underground anarchists a likely candidate for a frameup communists refused to turn over his body to his family for burial and rumors persisted that he had in fact died of torture
