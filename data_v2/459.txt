abdulredha mohamed hasan buhmaid or buhamaid was a 28 year old bahraini protester shot by a live bullet in head on 18 february 2011 he died in hospital three days later seventh death in bahraini uprising buhmaid was among a group of protesters who on 18 february marched toward pearl roundabout following funeral procession of protester ali abdulhadi mushaima who was killed four days earlier when protesters neared pearl roundabout army opened fire buhmaid collapsed to ground and blood poured from his head after it was hit by a bullet army opened fire twice more protesters regrouped after each round of shooting riot police finally intervened and dispersed protesters over one hundred protesters were injured some seriously buhmaid was taken to salmaniya hospital where attempts to revive him failed over course of three days he died on afternoon of 21 february several witnesses including journalists and medics accused authorities of shooting directly at protesters preventing some ambulances from reaching site and firing at others government however denied those statements it stated that warning shots were fired in air and accused protesters of faking injuries an investigation by a government appointed commission of inquiry blamed army for buhmaid s death incident marked first time that royal bahraini army was used to confront civilians and at time was considered bloodiest
