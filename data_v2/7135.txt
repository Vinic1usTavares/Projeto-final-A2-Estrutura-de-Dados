goofus and gallant is an american children s comic strip appearing monthly in highlights for children comic contrasts actions of titular characters presenting gallant s actions as right and good and goofus s as wrong and bad created by garry cleveland myers and first published in children s activities in 1940 goofus and gallant moved to highlights for children when magazine was founded in 1946 throughout its history goofus and gallant has been interpreted as an effective didactic comic it has been used in several studies as a stimulus to prompt children to identify kind and unkind actions
