air marshal sir richard williams 3 august february 1980 is widely regarded as father of royal australian air force raaf he was first military pilot trained in australia and went on to command australian and british fighter units in world war i a proponent for air power independent of other branches of armed services williams played a leading role in establishment of raaf and became its
