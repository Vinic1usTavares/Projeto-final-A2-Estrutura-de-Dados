tramp dentists is a 1913 american silent short comedy film released by newly formed joker productions by universal film manufacturing company directed by allen curtis film s cast includes max asher lee morris eddie boland joseph singleton and bobby vernon film is centered on two tramps dusty and weary who take over a dentist shop and get rich through their untrained dentistry extracting teeth with pincers and ice tongs after earning a large amount of money tramps return to their old way of life released on october 29 1913 this film was second joker comedy film received some criticism for its vulgar humor film had a wide national release in united states but film is presumed lost plot two tramps named dusty and weary awake from their slumber in a hay stock and are overcome with thirst two drink from a horse trough and dusty complains of toothache two go to dentist office only to be kicked out when dentist departs on a cruise two tramps then pose as dentists and occupy office and pull teeth with pincers and tongs when rightful owners return they drive off again tramps continue in their venture and quickly make a large sum of money before they desire their old way of life two tramps then surrender office to its rightful owners cast max asher lee morris eddie boland joseph singleton bobby vernon credited as de jardins production term tramp dentist refers to an unskilled
