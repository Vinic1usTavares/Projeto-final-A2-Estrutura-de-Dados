2008 and enlarged in 2011 it covers a forest area of and is largest wildlife sanctuary in tamil nadu in 2013 it became fourth tiger reserve as a part of project tiger in state of tamil nadu sathyamangalam forest range is a significant wildlife corridor in nilgiri biosphere reserve between western ghats and rest of eastern ghats and a genetic link between four other protected areas which
