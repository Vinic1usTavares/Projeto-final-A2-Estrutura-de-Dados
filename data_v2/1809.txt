charles edward chad mottola born october 15 1971 is an american professional baseball player and coach for tampa bay rays of major league baseball mlb mottola played in mlb for five years as an outfielder considered a journeyman mottola played professionally from 1992 through 2007 appearing in 59 mlb games and 1 801 minor league games he was hitting coach for toronto blue jays during 2013 season but his contract was not renewed for 2014 mottola is an alumnus of university of central florida ucf where he played college baseball for ucf knights baseball team a highly regarded prospect mottola was selected by cincinnati reds with fifth overall selection of 1992 mlb draft one spot ahead of derek jeter mottola played in minor league baseball for different organizations receiving major league playing time with cincinnati reds in 1996 toronto blue jays in 2000 and 2006 florida marlins in 2001 and baltimore orioles in 2004 as he received less playing time in 2007 he began to mentor younger teammates which helped him transition into a coaching career career amateur career mottola attended st thomas aquinas high school in fort lauderdale florida graduating in 1989 after leading
