attack on german flatts september 17 1778 was a raid on frontier settlement of german flatts new york which then also encompassed what is now herkimer during american revolutionary war attack was made by a mixed force of loyalists and iroquois under overall command of mohawk leader joseph brant and resulted in destruction of houses barns and crops and taking of livestock for raiders use settlers warned by heroic run of adam helmer took refuge in local forts but were too militarily weak to stop raiders brant s attack was one of a series executed under his command or that of loyalist and seneca leaders against communities on what was then frontier of western new york and northern pennsylvania new york authorities responded by ordering an expedition that destroyed brant s forward operating bases in iroquois territory background with failure of british general john burgoyne s campaign to hudson after battles of saratoga in october 1777 american revolutionary war in upstate new york
