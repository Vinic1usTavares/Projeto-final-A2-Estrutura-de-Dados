myron sidney january 23 1929 february 27 2008 known professionally as myron cope was an american sports journalist radio personality and sportscaster he is best known for being voice of pittsburgh steelers cope was a color commentator for steelers radio broadcasts for 35 years he was known for his distinctive nasally voice with an identifiable pittsburgh accent idiosyncratic speech pattern and a level of excitement rarely exhibited in broadcast booth cope s most notable catch phrase was yoi cope was first football announcer inducted into national radio hall of fame cope s autobiography double yoi was published in 2002 education and career born in pittsburgh pennsylvania to jewish parents of lithuanian ancestry cope graduated from taylor allderdice high school in 1947 and was inducted into their alumni hall of fame in 2009 he also graduated from university of pittsburgh he was originally a journalist before becoming a broadcaster his first job was in erie pennsylvania with daily times and by summer of 1951 he was working for pittsburgh post gazette cope then became a freelance journalist most notably for sports illustrated saturday evening post and pittsburgh post gazette in 1963 cope received e p dutton prize for best magazine sportswriting in nation for a portrayal of cassius clay cope spent 1983 college football season as a color analyst for pittsburgh panthers in 1987 he was named by hearst corporation as a noted literary achiever along with mark twain jack london frederic remington walter winchell and sidney sheldon at its 50th anniversary sports illustrated selected cope s profile of howard cosell as one of 50 best written works ever published in magazine family life cope married mildred lindberg of charleston in 1965 and couple moved to mt lebanon in 1972 copes moved to nearby upper st clair mildred died on september 20 1994 in 1999 cope moved back to mt lebanon to a condo in woodridge neighborhood he remained there until his final days when he entered a mt lebanon nursing home cope had three children elizabeth martha ann and daniel martha ann died shortly after her birth his son daniel was born with severe autism he has lived most of his life at allegheny valley school an institution specializing in intellectual developmental disabilities cope devoted much of his time and energy to pittsburgh causes addressing autism and spoke candidly about his experiences as parent of a child with autism and his efforts to better educate public at large about autism steelers broadcasting right thumb cope waves a terrible towel at heinz field october 31 2005 in 1968 cope began doing daily sports commentaries on what was then wtae am radio in pittsburgh he then took over a nightly talk show on station in 1973 his unique nasal voice with a distinctive pittsburgh area accent was noticed by steelers brass and he made his debut as a member of steelers radio team in 1970 during cope s 35 year broadcasting career with steelers longest term with a single team in nfl history he was accompanied by only two play by play announcers jack fleming with whom he broadcast until 1994 and bill hillgrove in keeping with his comic personality a series of television commentaries on wtae tv saw cope calling himself doctor cope and wearing a white lab coat while pretending to examine opposing team s strengths and weaknesses his predictor was known as cope ra scope catchphrases and nicknames like other sports announcers in pittsburgh particularly penguins commentator mike lange and late pirates announcer bob prince cope had a repertoire of unique catchphrases employed in his broadcasts such as mmm hah and his version of okey dokey cope often used yiddish expressions especially and yoi sometimes multiplied as double yoi or rarely triple yoi cope also created nicknames for many players and opposing teams it was cope who popularized bus as a nickname for former steelers running back jerome bettis jack splat for jack lambert and he gave kordell stewart nickname slash cope also used term cincinnati to describe their division rivals known during 1990s for a string of bad seasons and numerous draft busts terrible towel cope played a large role in invention of terrible towel needing a way to excite fans during a 1975 playoff game against baltimore colts cope urged fans to take yellow dish towels to game and wave them throughout originally cope wanted to sell rubber jack lambert masks but realizing high costs for masks opted for inexpensive option for terrible towel terrible towel has gained much popularity since its invention and is arguably best known fan symbol of any major pro sports team in 1996 cope gave rights to terrible towel to allegheny valley school in pennsylvania school provides care for more than 900 people with intellectual disabilities and physical disabilities including cope s son who has severe autism proceeds from terrible towel have helped raise 3 million for school retirement and death
