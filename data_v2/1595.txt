magnapop is an american rock band based in atlanta georgia formed in 1989 band has consistently included songwriting duo linda hopper as vocalist and ruthie morris on guitar magnapop first achieved recognition in benelux countries of belgium netherlands and luxembourg through festival circuit and have remained popular in europe throughout their career after modest success in united states in mid 1990s with singles slowly slowly and open door and a series of albums produced by michael stipe bob mould and geza x band went on an extended hiatus due to dissolution of their record label they returned with a new rhythm section in 2005 on daemon records release mouthfeel band has continued to perform and record since this reunion and have self released two more albums magnapop s musical style is noted for blending pop vocals and melodies of hopper with aggressive punk influenced guitar playing of morris and her back up vocal harmonies history formation and self titled debut 1989 1993
