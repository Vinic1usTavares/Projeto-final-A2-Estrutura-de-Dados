john t j t white july 10 1920 november 21 2005 was a college football assistant coach and a second team 1947 college football all american center who played for national championship teams at both university of michigan and ohio state university white also played basketball for ohio state buckeyes men s basketball team although white was drafted to play professional football he chose to pursue a career as an assistant football coach for both michigan wolverines and penn state nittany lions football teams he served as an assistant coach for a national champion at michigan and three undefeated and untied seasons at penn state white served in united states army during world war ii causing a break in his collegiate education personal white was born in wadley georgia and raised in river rouge michigan white earned his bachelor s degree in education from michigan in 1948 he earned his master s degree in education at michigan white married former verna mcqueen and they had one son brian verna and white were married for 53 years before she died at 75 in october 1997 of emphysema athletic career at river rouge high school he played baseball basketball and football before attending ohio state in 1942 he lettered in both basketball and football at ohio state as a sophomore he was a player for national champions on both 1942 ohio state buckeyes football team and 1947 michigan wolverines football team he is one of only three players along with justin boren and howard to have played for football teams of both michigan and ohio state this low number can be attributed to strong rivalry between two schools white matriculated at ohio state university where he earned a varsity letter playing for paul brown s 1942 team white played at ohio state for two years he served in united states army from 1943 to 1945 during world war ii and lettered two years for fritz crisler s michigan teams his younger brother paul was captain at michigan when white transferred j t was starting center for 1946 and 1947 teams football writers association of america named him as their second team all american center for 1947 college football season white played in 1948 college all star game and he was drafted by two pro teams detroit lions with first pick of 21st round 186th overall in 1947 nfl draft and brooklyn dodgers of all america football conference coaching career he chose to join bennie oosterbaan s michigan coaching staff for six years 1948 53 including national champion 1948 michigan wolverines football team then for 26 seasons from 1954 through 1979 j t was a defensive line coach at penn state first under rip engle and then under joe paterno who took over in 1966 when white moved to penn state to work for engle staff included paterno earle bruce sever jim o hora and jim patrick his tenure on staff included three undefeated and untied seasons 1968 1969 and 1973 subsequently from 1980 1982 he served as assistant to penn state athletic director see also
