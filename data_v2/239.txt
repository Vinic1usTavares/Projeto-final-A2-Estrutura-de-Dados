sol arena y mar is a song written by arturo perez francisco loyo and salo loyo and co written produced and performed by mexican singer luis miguel it is a horn driven uptempo pop song with jazz influence which deals with a strained relationship song s lyrics were rumored to have been influenced by miguel s previous relationship with daisy fuentes it was released as lead single from album amarte es un placer on 19 july 1999 song received mixed reviews from music critics several reviewers complimented miguel s delivery and favorably compared it to uptempo tracks from aries 1993 while others criticized its horn arrangements and lyrics it received a bmi latin award in 2001 single peaked at number three on billboard hot latin songs chart in united states number two in spain and reached number one in argentina chile and mexico and sold over 50 000 copies since its release background and composition in 1997 luis miguel released his twelfth studio album romances third record in his romance series on which he covers classic latin american boleros it sold over 4 5 million copies and won grammy award for best latin pop performance in 1998 to promote romances he embarked on a tour of united states latin america and spain lasting over a year by 1998 miguel was considered most popular latin artist internationally and his albums had sold over 35 million copies worldwide miguel began a relationship with american singer mariah carey following year after an absence of two years on music scene miguel announced on 19 july 1999 that he would release a new album by september on same day he also released album s first single sol arena y mar four versions of single were released which includes album version and three remixes done by danny saber he said upcoming album would be a return to pop as opposed to bolero cover versions he had recorded on romance series he also denied rumors that he was planning to record a duet with carey album s final title amarte es un placer was announced on 17 august 1999 miguel confirmed that it was first album where he was more involved in tracks composition in addition to miguel co writing several of record s tracks he was assisted by other composers including armando manzanero juan carlos calderon and arturo perez recording took place at a m studios cello studios ocean way recording and record plant in hollywood promis felt horn arrangements gave song a jazzy sophisticated adult leaning feel john lannert of billboard was unimpressed with track calling it a vapid uptempo number about a downtrodden affair and criticized its weary arrangements and uninspired musicianship houston chronicle critic joey guerra chided tune as a tepid mix of blaring horns and uninspired lyrics newsday editor richard torres stated that miguel s vocals provides perfect counterpoint to peppy horn arrangement on song leila cobo of miami herald editor noted that sol arena y mar was similar to uptempo tracks from aries dallas morning news critic mario tarradell called sol arena y mar a guilty pleasure and found it to be most reminiscent of catchy fare on 1993 s wonderful aries ernesto lechner wrote for los angeles times that song lacks sophistication that defines most pop today ramiro burr of san antonio express news praised track stating that sensuous horns are marvelous sweeping vocal harmonies enticing but it s luis miguel s piercing tenor that grabs you sol arena y mar was recognized as one of best performing latin songs of year at 2001 bmi latin awards in united states sol arena y mar debuted at number eight on billboard hot latin songs chart on week of 21 august 1999 it peaked at number three on chart two weeks later track also peaked at number two on latin pop songs chart with number one position
