new york state route 360 ny 360 was an east west state highway located in northwestern monroe county new york in united states it extended for through town of hamlin from an intersection with ny 272 at monroe orleans county line to a junction with ny 19 north of hamlet of hamlin ny 360 intersected former southern terminus of ny 215 east of county line at its northern junction with redman road most of ny 360 passed through rural areas however easternmost portion of route was located in a residential neighborhood that comprises northernmost portion of hamlet of hamlin highways that ny 360 followed were originally improved to state highway standards in 1900s and 1910s and first designated as part of ny 18 as part of 1930 renumbering of state highways in new york by following year ny 360 designation was assigned to what is now ny 18 between ny 272 and ny 19 alignments of ny 18 and ny 360 between those two routes were flipped ownership and maintenance of ny 360 was transferred from state of new york to monroe county on november 26 2007 as part of a highway maintenance swap between two levels of government ny 360 overlapped with four different county routes at various points
