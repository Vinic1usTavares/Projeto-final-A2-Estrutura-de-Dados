karnan is a 1964 indian tamil language hindu mythological film produced and directed by b r panthulu it features sivaji ganesan leading an ensemble cast consisting of n t rama rao s a ashokan r muthuraman devika savitri and m v rajamma film is based on story of karna a character from hindu epic mahabharata he is born to an unmarried mother kunti who abandoned him then discovered and adopted by a charioteer karnan does not want to follow his foster father s profession and instead becomes a warrior he then befriends duryodhana kaurava prince eventually setting initial grounds of kurukshetra war where he will join duryodhana to fight against his own half brothers pandavas karnan which was officially launched in 1963 was shot in palaces at jaipur and war sequences were filmed in kurukshetra which featured several soldiers from indian army film s original soundtrack was composed by duo viswanathan ramamoorthy while lyrics were written by kannadasan screenplay was written by a s nagarajan and dialogues by sakthi t k krishnasamy karnan was released on 14 january 1964 during festival occasion of pongal film ran for over 100 days in theatres and later won certificate of merit for third best feature film at 11th national film awards it was considered a milestone in tamil cinema as it brought together then leading actors of south indian cinema ganesan and rama rao film was also partly responsible for a resurgence in hindu purana films in industry a digitised version of karnan was released in march 2012 to critical and commercial success eventually establishing a trend of re releasing digitised versions of old films in tamil cinema plot unmarried princess kunti is seen taking away a small coffin with a baby boy she earlier got a boon from durvasa for her pious
