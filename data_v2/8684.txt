2nd destroyer flotilla df in 1914 when first world war began in august of that year 2nd df was assigned to grand fleet where their primary task was to protect fleet from submarines by beginning of based in ireland by beginning of 1918 but was soon transferred to mediterranean fleet and based in gibraltar for rest of war ship was reduced to reserve in 1919 and was sold for scrap following year
