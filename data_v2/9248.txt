torreon massacre was a racially motivated massacre that took place on 13 15 may 1911 in mexican city of torreon coahuila over 300 chinese residents were killed by a local mob and revolutionary forces of francisco i madero a large number of chinese homes and establishments were looted and destroyed torreon was last major city to be taken by maderistas during mexican revolution when government forces withdrew rebels entered city in early morning and along with local population began a ten hour massacre of chinese community event touched off a diplomatic crisis between china and mexico with former demanding 30 million pesos in reparation at one point it was rumored that china had even dispatched a warship to mexican waters cruiser hai chi which was anchored in cuba at time an investigation into massacre concluded that it was an unprovoked act of racial hatred background chinese immigration to mexico began as early as 17th century with a number settling in mexico city immigration increased when mexican president porfirio diaz attempted to encourage foreign investment and tourism to boost country s economy two countries signed a treaty of amity and commerce in 1899
