william tailer february 25 1675 6 march 1 1731 2 was a military officer and politician in province of massachusetts bay born into wealthy and influential stoughton family he twice married into other politically powerful families he served as lieutenant governor of province from 1711 until 1716 and again in early 1730s during each of these times he was briefly acting governor he was a political opponent of governor joseph dudley and was a supporter of a land bank proposal intended to address province s currency problems during his first tenure as acting governor he authorized erection of boston light earliest lighthouse in what is now united states he was active in provincial defense and commanded a regiment in 1710 siege of port royal capital of french acadia during queen anne s war he was responsible for overseeing defenses of boston in 1720s and was sent to negotiate with iroquois and abenaki during dummer s war jonathan belcher initially a political opponent later became an
