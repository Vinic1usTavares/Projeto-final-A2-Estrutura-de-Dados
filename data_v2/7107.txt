life also known as we are odst is a television and cinema advertisement launched in 2009 by microsoft to promote first person shooter halo 3 odst in united states 150 second piece follows a young soldier through enlistment training and battle as an orbital drop shock trooper odst analogous to a paratrooper that drops from space to a battlefield life was created by advertising agency t a g an offshoot of mccann erickson production of commercial itself was handled by production company morton zander mjz it was directed by rupert sanders and post production was conducted by asylum it was filmed in hungary just outside budapest in a coal mine and abandoned factories to give sequence an eastern bloc aesthetic commercial and its associated campaign proved hugely successful on week of its launch halo 3 odst became top selling game for xbox 360 worldwide and over 2 5 million copies were sold within first few weeks of release life went on to win a number of honours from advertising and entertainment industries including two clio awards a london international advertising award and several honours from cannes lions international advertising festival most prestigious awards ceremony in director rupert sanders known for his work with production company mjz on campaigns such as lava for guinness and great return for nike inc was given five weeks to produce commercial with three days set aside for filming several locations were scouted for shooting with an eye towards an eastern bloc aesthetic these included several areas around chernobyl ukraine however tight schedule precluded using locations too distant from one another for different scenes and in end three spots outside of budapest hungary were settled upon cooling tower of an active nuclear power station was dressed to act as backdrop to opening funeral scene an open pit coal mine served as setting for battle scenes and an abandoned aluminum factory was used to stage closing funeral scene sanders drew inspiration for shooting style and aesthetic of commercial from a variety of sources including news footage from journalists embedded with military forces in afghanistan and russian feature films such as stalker and come and see to keep details within commercial consistent with established halo canon creators of halo series bungie provided sanders with information on aspects ranging from appropriate fur color and rank insignia for brute to armor and weaponry of odst soldiers post production with filming complete mjz contacted post production company asylum to begin work on substantial visual effects component of life team led by visual effects supervisor robert moggach tripled in size as scale of work required became apparent given deadline of three weeks work on opening funeral scene was relatively easy requiring creation of only minor elements such as additional tombstones in foreground and color correction on actors same was true for training sequence where only minimal tracking work and compositing of matte backgrounds was required bulk of visual effects work was in battle sequence this ranged from simple work such as wire removal to creation of dynamic lighting and reflections from visors and armor to particle effects for background smoke and dust plasma weapon fire and alterations to some of filmed explosions to lend them appearance given to plasma explosions within halo games entire background was a 3d projection of matte paintings and dramatic skies while brute was partially animatronic substantial adjustments such as scaling to almost twice size and enhancement of fine muscle movements of creature were made other elements such as banshee aircraft and drop pods were created entirely through use of cgi software used by asylum fx included flame and nuke for compositing maya for animation renderman and mantra for rendering for tracking silhouette pro for rotoscoping work music life was scored by gareth williams a composer for human worldwide music an arrangement of light of aidan s lament was created specifically for ad and featured a wide variety of instruments percussive elements included military snare drums a hand drum samoan log
