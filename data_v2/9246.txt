hockey league khl prior to 2012 filatov played in north america for ottawa senators and columbus blue jackets of national hockey league nhl at 2008 nhl entry draft filatov was selected sixth overall situation in columbus and was loaned to cska moscow for remainder of season at 2011 nhl entry draft blue jackets then traded him to ottawa in exchange for a third round draft pick in december 2011 senators loaned filatov to cska moscow for balance of 2011 12 season following season filatov signed with salavat yulaev senators chose not to tender filatov a qualifying offer making him a free agent
