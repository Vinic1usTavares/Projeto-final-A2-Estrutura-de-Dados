aluna sagita gutawa born 11 august 1993 in jakarta better known as gita gutawa is an indonesian soprano actress and songwriter she is also daughter of composer erwin gutawa although she originally studied piano gutawa later switched to vocals she was discovered in 2004 while practising her vocals then booked to sing a duet with ada band after heaven of love 2005 featuring duet sold 800 000 copies gutawa was approached by sony music indonesia with an offer to record a solo album success of that album self titled gita gutawa led to numerous acting and marketing job offers since her debut she has recorded two more albums well received harmoni cinta in 2009 and islamic themed balada shalawat in 2010 as an actress she has starred in two indonesian soap operas ajari aku cinta and ajari lagi aku cinta she voiced a character for animated meraih mimpi and made her feature film debut in 2010 s love in perth personality wise dian of jakarta post has described gutawa as being sweet like cotton candy and warm like a kiss however gutawa has noted that there are people who believe she succeeded through nepotism gutawa has received several awards including best newcomer at 2008 anugerah musik indonesia she also won 6th international nile children song festival in cairo biography early life thumb gita gutawa with her father erwin 2008 gutawa was born aluna sagita gutawa aluna sagita meaning song rhythm in jakarta indonesia on 11 august 1993 to erwin gutawa a composer and first child of couple she enjoyed watching her father work on his compositions as a result he enrolled her in performed in public for first time after chrisye who often collaborated with her father invited her on stage she later described him as her idol music career in 2004 gutawa was approached by manager accepted duet titled yang best for you was included on ada band s 2005 album heaven of love which sold 800 000 copies after singing other duets with different singers including religious singer haddad gutawa was asked to record a solo album by sony music indonesia after a year in production gutawa s self titled debut album was released in february 2007 album was produced by her father and featured songs written by glenn and melly goeslaw well received gita gutawa sold 150 000 copies in four months and was eventually certified triple platinum after success of album gutawa s previously school listing she eventually wrote five of album s twelve songs album was released in may 2009 selling well in august of that year she released her biography kota musik gita gutawa gita gutawa s music box
