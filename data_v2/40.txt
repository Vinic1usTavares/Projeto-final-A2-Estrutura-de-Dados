rudra mahalaya temple also known as is a destroyed desecrated temple complex at siddhpur in patan district of gujarat india its construction was started in 943 ad by mularaja and completed in 1140 ad by jayasimha siddharaja rulers of chaulukya dynasty this hindu temple was destroyed by sultan of delhi alauddin khalji and later sultan of gujarat ahmed shah i 1410 44 desecrated and substantially demolished this temple and also converted part of it into congregational mosque jami masjid of city two porches and four pillars of former central structure still stand along with western part of complex used as a congregational mosque history thumb four pillars and architrave of ruined rudra mahalaya temple left siddhpur historically known as sristhal under rulers of chaulukya dynasty was a prominent town in 10th century in tenth century 943 ad mularaja founder of chaulukya dynasty of gujarat started erection of rudra mahalay temple in his youth had slain his maternal uncle usurped his throne and murdered whole of his mother s kindred and in old age his crimes hung heavily on his mind he made pilgrimages and courted favour of brahmins from far and near to a band of them he gave sristhal and committing kingdom to his son he retired there to end his days in their company 996 ad but rudra mahalay was still incomplete nor was it finished till 1140 ad an inscription and ballad regarding its construction says it was during 12th century in 1140 ad that jayasimha siddharaja 1094 1144 consecrated temple complex and it became principal temple complex of siddhpur according to another legend two from malwa named and took up their haunt among rush grass that covered neighbourhood of rudra mahalaya and lived by plunder there they found foundations of a temple and shiva linga and said that in night they had seen heavenly beings this was told to and led to erection or completion of temple in i ahmadi ali muhammad khan writes king on signifying his intention of building temple requested astrologers it is said to appoint a fortunate hour and they at this time predicted destruction of building then siddha raja caused images of horse lords and other great kings to be placed in temple and near them a representation of himself in attitude of supplication with an inscription praying that even if land was laid waste this temple might not be destroyed alauddin khalji sent an army under khan and nusrat khan who dismantled temple complex in 1296 ad samvat 1353 temple was further destroyed and western part of it was converted into congregational mosque jami mosque by
