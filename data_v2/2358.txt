worms armageddon is a 2d artillery turn based tactics video game developed by team17 and part of worms series player controls a team of up to eight worms in combat against opposing teams either ai or player controlled using weapons both based on real life weapons and ones that are cartoonish player can customize battles teams and maps for near infinite gameplay options originally meant to be an expansion pack to worms 2 and initially titled wormageddon worms armageddon was released as a standalone game initially for pcs in 1999 and has since been ported to other platforms game was also of worms world party with identical graphics gameplay is turn based with each team moving in sequence which is determined randomly across two dimensional terrain during a single turn a team can only move one of their worms unless an item that allows team to select their worm is used worms can walk and jump as well as when proper items are available swing by rope parachute teleport and bungee objective of a traditional match is to defeat all opposing teams by killing their worms although in campaign some missions have other objectives such as collecting a specific crate each worm begins round with a specific amount of health which is predefined by chosen game options or by scripting in campaign levels when hit with a weapon worm will lose health depending upon power of weapon and directness of hit a worm can be killed either by exploding after having its health reduced to zero or by being knocked into water around and below level game includes a wide variety of weapons including melee projectile and explosive weapons as well as airstrike based attacks some are based on real life arms such as shotgun bazooka and hand grenade others are rather fanciful and cartoonish such as sheep which serves as a mobile explosive and skunk which releases poisonous gas in a normal match all teams begin with same weapons based on chosen weapon set some weapons may not become available until a certain number of turns pass depending on game options additional weapons may randomly fall onto terrain in airdropped and teleported crates in addition to normal weapons during team creation each team chooses a special weapon which becomes available to them after a certain number of turns special weapons are more powerful than regular weapons and often offer special abilities super weapons will rarely fall in weapon crates these weapons are often based on cartoonish themes such as french sheep strike and usually devastating in power in homage to film monty python and holy grail one of game weapons is a holy hand grenade with a sound effect reminiscent of hallelujah chorus from handel s messiah gameplay modes worms armageddon includes a series of training missions a single player campaign with premade missions a deathmatch mode in which player fights increasingly difficult and outnumbered battles against computer local multiplayer and online multiplayer local multiplayer allows player to select which teams participate in battle as well as number of worms and handicaps which options and weapons are used and level to be played on matches can use any separately in movement rotation but share weapons and score if multiple human players are using same computer game functions in a hotseat mode online multiplayer is set up similarly to local ranking system for players customization worms armageddon includes a very high level of customizability in multiplayer games or skirmishes player can create custom game modes with preferred gameplay weapons will fall in crates and how often and how powerful individual weapons are worms armageddon offers several levels of customizability outside of direct gameplay options game offers players
