2013 cecafa cup known as gotv cecafa challenge cup for sponsorship reasons was 37th edition of annual cecafa cup an international football competition consisting of national teams of member nations of council for east and central africa football associations cecafa tournament was held in kenya from 27 november to 12 december contested by twelve national teams tournament had zambia invited as a guest nation to fill in for djibouti who failed to participate for a second consecutive year this followed tournament s recent tradition of inviting other african nations to take part once a cecafa nation pulled out malawi were invited to take part in previous edition held in uganda while they and zimbabwe took part in 2011 after eritrea withdrew from competition hosts kenya ran out 2 0 winners in final against three time champions sudan with a brace from allan securing stars their sixth title after losing to previous hosts uganda at same stage previous year third place playoff was won by guests zambia who beat tanzania 6 5 on penalties after playing out to a 1 1 draw after 90 minutes background and sponsorship on 25 november 2012 it was announced that kenya was chosen to host tournament after bids were placed by host nation as well as rwanda however their hosting of tournament was not confirmed until a congress meeting for confederation of african football held in marrakesh morocco on 11 march 2013 after it was announced that football kenya federation had met all requirements for hosting tournaments with support from kenyan government capital city nairobi mombasa kisumu and nakuru were identified as host cities for tournament on 21 november 2013 cecafa announced that gotv would be title sponsors of tournament having committed ksh 11 25 million towards organisation of event and a further ksh 45 million towards broadcasting of all 26 matches other companies that were sponsoring tournament were coca cola who committed ksh 6 5 million and uap insurance
