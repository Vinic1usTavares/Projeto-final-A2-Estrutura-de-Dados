walden wallkill rail trail also known as jesse mchugh rail trail is a rail trail between village of walden new york and neighboring hamlet of wallkill two communities are located in orange and ulster counties respectively in upstate new york trail like wallkill valley rail trail to north is part of former wallkill valley railroad s rail corridor land was purchased by towns of montgomery and shawangunk in 1985 and converted to a public trail portion of trail in shawangunk was formally opened in 1993 and named after former town supervisor jesse mchugh plans to pave trail between walden and wallkill were discussed since 2001 and route was finally paved between 2008 and 2009 trail includes an unofficial unimproved section to north of wallkill and is bounded by ny 52 and ny 208 history
