during world war i banker was chosen to head a team of thirty three telephone operators of telephone unit no 1 for telephone operations assigned to war duty in france this was first group of women who were given popular name hello girls banker sailed with her team members from new jersey on 6 march 1918 to take up assignment as chief operator for first army headquarters in paris after arriving with her team in england group set sail by ferry across english channel however bad weather in form of thick fog prevented ferry from reaching french shores and it had to be anchored a few miles away to wait for fog to lift this location made vessel an easy target for german bombing at that time one vessel out of four had suffered bombing and team members remained at full readiness to evacuate vessel at short notice women s group stayed on deck in open for forty eight continuous hours this situation did not dishearten banker or her team members and as banker later said what good sports girls were in that first unit they took everything in their stride they were pioneers on arrival in paris banker and her team were posted to headquarters of advance section in chaumont sur haute marne which was
