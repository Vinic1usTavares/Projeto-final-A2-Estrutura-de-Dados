arthur knights of justice was first enix game developed by an american company manley associates in issaquah washington roughly two dozen people worked on game though not all at same time it was initially planned for a 16 megabit cartridge but four additional megabits were eventually added to expand game development spanned about two years in addition to original cartoon series developers gathered ideas from several sources for inspiration including legend of zelda action adventure game series and books such as t h white s book of merlyn and fables from medieval poet marie de france they noted that hardest part of development was coming up with puzzles for each of regions as they had to be fun and challenging but not repetitive while they tried to maintain a balance between action and puzzles they noted that they focused more on puzzle aspect of game favorite parts of game for developers include dragon battles boss blackwing and morgana s warlords reception title
