djaoeh dimata perfected spelling jauh dimata indonesian for out of sight is a 1948 film from what is now indonesia written and directed by andjar asmara for south pacific film corporation spfc starring ratna asmara and ali joego it follows a woman who goes to jakarta to find work after her husband is blinded in an accident spfc s first production djaoeh dimata took two to three months to film and cost almost 130 000 gulden first domestically produced feature film to be released in five years djaoeh dimata received favourable reviews although financially it was outperformed by roestam sutan palindih s air mata mengalir di tjitarum released soon after film s cast remained active in indonesian film industry some for another thirty years and spfc produced six more works before closing in 1949 a copy of film is stored at sinematek indonesia plot upright 1 35 thumb ratna asmara ali joego and sukarno in a scene from film a poor villager asrad ali joego is blinded following a traffic
