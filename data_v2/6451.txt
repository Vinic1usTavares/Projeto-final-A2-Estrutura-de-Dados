originating lyric baritone roles in gilbert and sullivan operas from 1877 to 1896 his performing career spanned more than four decades he also wrote at least a dozen works for stage walters p 21 fn 25 after two years with a comic touring company barrington joined richard d oyly carte s opera company and over next two decades created a number of memorable comic opera roles including captain corcoran in h m s pinafore 1878 sergeant of police in pirates of penzance 1880 and pooh bah in mikado 1885 among many others failing in an 1888 attempt to become a theatrical manager barrington refocused his energies on acting and occasional playwriting beginning in 1896 and continuing for ten years barrington played in a series of very successful musical comedies under management of george edwardes at daly s theatre specialising in comic portrayals of pompous rulers or other persons of authority one of most popular features of his performances was his insertion of topical songs or verses of songs into these musical comedies after leaving daly s he continued to appear in musical comedy roles and performed in music hall he also essayed a few shakespeare and other dramatic roles and appeared in a
