uss constellation vs l insurgente or action of 9 february 1799 was a single ship action fought between frigates of french navy and united states navy during quasi war an undeclared war that lasted from 1798 to 1800 battle resulted in capture of l insurgente after an intense firefight in which both sides exchanged heavy broadsides and musket fire french privateering attacks against american vessels begun a year prior caused conflict between united states and france an american squadron under commodore thomas truxtun had been sent to patrol caribbean waters between puerto rico and saint kitts with orders to engage any french forces they found in area while truxtun was sailing independently of his squadron in constellation his flagship he met and engaged l insurgente after chasing french ship through a storm constellation forced l insurgente into an engagement that lasted an hour and fourteen minutes before french frigate surrendered french sustained heavy casualties in action while numbers of american dead and wounded were low after action l insurgente was taken to saint kitts and commissioned into united states navy as with this and later victories american morale soared
