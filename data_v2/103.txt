vettor pisani class consisted of two armoured cruisers built for royal italian navy regia marina in 1890s two ships of class vettor pisani and carlo alberto were frequently deployed overseas during their careers former served in far east during boxer rebellion of 1900 while latter was involved in pioneering long range radio experiments several years later before deploying to south american waters carlo alberto then served as a training ship for several years both ships participated in italo turkish war of 1911 12 and played minor roles in world war i during which time carlo alberto was converted into a troop transport and vettor pisani into a repair ship they were both discarded in 1920 and subsequently scrapped design and description thumb left right elevation and plan drawing of
