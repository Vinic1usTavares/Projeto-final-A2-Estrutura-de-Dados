theodor weissenberger 21 december 1914 11 june 1950 was a german luftwaffe military aviator during world war ii and a fighter ace credited with 208 enemy aircraft shot down in 375 combat missions majority of his victories were claimed near arctic ocean in northern sector of eastern front but he also claimed 33 victories over western front he claimed eight of these victories over western allies while flying messerschmitt me 262 jet fighter born in muhlheim am main in german empire weissenberger who had been a glider pilot in his youth volunteered for service in luftwaffe of nazi germany in 1936 following flight training he was posted to heavy fighter squadron of jagdgeschwader 77 jg 77 77th fighter wing in 1941 he claimed his first aerial victory over norway on 24 october 1941 after 23 aerial victories as heavy fighter pilot he received german cross in gold and was then posted to jagdgeschwader 5 jg 5 5th fighter wing in september 1942 there he received knight s cross of iron cross on 13 november 1942 after 38 aerial victories in june 1943 weissenberger was appointed staffelkapitan of 7 staffel of jg 5 following his 112th aerial victory he was awarded knight s cross of iron cross with oak leaves on 2 august 1943 he was appointed staffelkapitan of 6 staffel in september 1943 and in march 1944 he was given command of ii gruppe of jg 5 which was operating in defense of reich missions in june 1944 he took command of i gruppe of jg 5 which defended against invasion of normandy weissenberger claimed 25 aerial victories in this theater which included his 200th victory
