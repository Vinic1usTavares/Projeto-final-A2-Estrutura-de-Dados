royals before returning to mexican league lopez was named 1977 mexican league most valuable player mvp he returned to major leagues with st louis cardinals in 1978 and pitched for detroit tigers between 1979 and 1985 lopez finished seventh in cy young award voting in 1979 he earned a 10 1 record and 14 saves for detroit s 1984 world series championship team lopez returned to postseason with more difficult to locate practice facilities for soccer he studied public administration at autonomous university of puebla lopez caught attention of scout ramon la chita garcia of mexico city reds in mexican league he debuted with team s minor league affiliate in las in 1967 he was promoted to parent club next season at age of 19 after starting 1969 season with mexico city lopez joined minatitlan 1975 during 1977 mexican league season lopez earned a 19 3 win loss record a 2 01 era and 165 strikeouts over 157 innings in 73 games despite being a closer he was fifth in league in wins and he set a new single season save record 30 he was named league s mvp for several years in mid to late 1970s lopez pitched winter baseball in mexican pacific league he broke league records by leading league in
