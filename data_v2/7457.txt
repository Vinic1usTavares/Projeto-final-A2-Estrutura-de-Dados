mackinac island is an island and resort area covering in land area in u s state of michigan it is located in lake huron at eastern end of straits of mackinac between state s upper and lower peninsulas island was home to an odawa settlement before european colonization began in 17th century it served a strategic position as a center on commerce of great lakes fur trade this led to establishment of fort mackinac on island by british during american revolutionary war it was site of two battles during war of 1812 in late 19th century mackinac island became a popular tourist attraction and summer colony much of island has undergone extensive historical preservation and restoration as a result entire island is listed as a national historic landmark it is well known for its numerous cultural events its wide variety of architectural styles including victorian grand hotel and its ban on almost all motor vehicles more than 80 percent of island is preserved as mackinac island state park
