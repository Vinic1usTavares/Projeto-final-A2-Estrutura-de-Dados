refugee olympic team competed at 2016 summer olympics in rio de janeiro brazil from 5 to 21 august 2016 as independent olympic participants in march 2016 international olympic committee ioc president thomas bach declared that ioc would choose five to ten refugees to compete at rio olympics in context of worldwide refugee crisis of which european migrant crisis is a prominent part athletes competed under olympic flag initially they were labeled team of refugee olympic athletes with ioc country code roa but this was updated to refugee olympic team with country code rot as part of an effort to
