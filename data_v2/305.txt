etienne d hastrel de rivedoux was a general of first french empire who fought in french revolutionary wars and napoleonic wars he was born 4 february 1766 at pointe aux trembles in quebec which was then british colony province of quebec son of an officer in french military his father had served in india during seven years war and later in quebec son of rural nobility he attended royal military school in paris as a gentleman cadet after his graduation hastrel occupied several junior positions during french revolution in 1789 and its subsequent political and social upheavals he affirmed his loyalty to france described by his colleagues as naturally talented quickly moved into ranks of general staff filling increasingly important positions in several french field armies including army of rhine army of danube army of helvetia and grande armee he also managed an autonomous division of engineers and sappers during peninsular war after bourbon restoration he retained his titles and honors he was recalled from retirement in 1830 during july revolution he died 19 september 1846 at versailles family etienne d hastrel descended from a family of rural notables marcel fournier fischier
