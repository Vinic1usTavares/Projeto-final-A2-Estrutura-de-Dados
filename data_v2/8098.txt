mary of teck victoria mary augusta louise olga pauline claudine agnes 26 may 1867 24 march 1953 was queen consort of united kingdom and british dominions and empress consort of india as wife of king george v although technically a princess of teck in kingdom of wurttemberg she was born and raised in united kingdom her parents were francis duke of teck who was of german extraction and princess mary adelaide of cambridge who was a granddaughter of king george iii she was informally known as may after her birth month at age of 24 she was betrothed to her second cousin once removed prince albert victor duke of clarence and avondale eldest son of prince of wales but six weeks after announcement of engagement he died unexpectedly during an influenza pandemic following year she became engaged to albert victor s only surviving brother george who subsequently became king before her husband s accession she was successively duchess of york duchess of cornwall and princess of wales as queen consort from 1910 she supported her husband through first world war his ill health and major political changes arising from aftermath of war after george s death in 1936 she became queen mother when her eldest son edward viii ascended throne but to her dismay he abdicated later same year in order to marry twice divorced american socialite wallis simpson she supported her second son george vi until his death in 1952 she died following year during reign of her granddaughter elizabeth ii who had not yet been crowned early life thumb left upright mary as an infant with her parents princess
