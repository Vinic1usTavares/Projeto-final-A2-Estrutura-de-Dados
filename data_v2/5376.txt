norsk spisevognselskap a s often abbreviated nss or shortened to spisevognselskapet was a norwegian state enterprise which operated restaurant carriages on norwegian trains and restaurants at railway stations and railway hotels company was established in december 1918 and started a catering service in 1919 originally owned by norwegian trunk railway it was acquired by state in 1926 meals served in restaurant carriages were relatively expensive although they were available to all passengers in 1950s company began using serving trolleys on trains in january 1975 nss merged with convenience store chain narvesen into a new company called narvesen spisevognselskapet this enterprise was partly owned by norwegian state railways nsb and fritt ord before it merged with reitan group and was delisted
