harvester and another destroyer sank a german submarine in october she was briefly assigned to force h in may 1941 but her anti aircraft armament was deemed too weak and she was transferred to newfoundland escort force in june 1941 for escort duties in north atlantic ship was returned to western approaches command in october 1941 and was converted to an escort destroyer in early 1942
