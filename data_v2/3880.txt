roles film follows journey of two strangers a jobless carefree man living in bangalore and a woman with whom he has fallen in love upon woman s request he drives her to mumbai while a group of gangsters follow them planning to kidnap woman meanwhile man also has a past in city and he goes there to sort it out film produced by n subash chandra bose under banner of thirupathi brothers and distributed by dhayanidhi alagiri s cloud nine movies features a film score and soundtrack composed by yuvan shankar raja cinematography by madhi and editing by anthony gonsalves following a lengthy pre production phase during which film underwent major changes in its main cast and its technical crew it began shooting in december 2008 at various locations throughout south india most notably in karnataka andhra pradesh and maharashtra film was released on 2 april 2010 to predominantly positive reviews and was commercially successful a telugu dubbed version of film titled awara was released
