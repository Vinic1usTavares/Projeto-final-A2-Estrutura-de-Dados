jane dudley nee guildford duchess of northumberland 1508 1509 1555 was an english noblewoman wife of john dudley 1st duke of northumberland and mother of guildford dudley and robert dudley 1st earl of leicester having grown up with her future husband who was her father s ward she married at about age 16 they had 13 children jane dudley served as a lady in waiting at court of henry viii and was a close friend of his final wife catherine parr reformed in religious outlook she was also a supporter of protestant martyr anne askew under young edward vi john dudley became one of most powerful politicians rising to be earl of warwick and later duke of northumberland after fall of lord protector somerset in 1549 john dudley joined forces with his wife to promote his rehabilitation and a reconciliation between their families which was symbolised by a marriage between their children in spring of 1553 duchess of northumberland became mother in law of lady jane grey whom duke of northumberland unsuccessfully tried to establish on english throne after death of edward vi mary i being victorious duchess sought frantically to save her husband s life notwithstanding his and her son guildford s executions she was successful in achieving release of rest of her family by befriending spanish noblemen who came to england with philip of spain she died soon afterwards aged 46
