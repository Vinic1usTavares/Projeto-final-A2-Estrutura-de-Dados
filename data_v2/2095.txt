but these were later replaced by channel for path to cessation of suffering and attainment of dhamma channel features many types of programs with buddhist content and has programs in several languages channel started in 2002 as a means to reach remote provinces in thailand controversially channel made international headlines in 2012 when it featured a teaching on afterlife of steve jobs on 26 december 2016 thai authorities withdrew permit for satellite channel permanently during legal investigations into temple by thai junta in april 2017 it was reported however that channel s programming
