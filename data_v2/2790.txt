john lansdale jr 9 january 1912 22 august 2003 was a united states army colonel who was in charge of intelligence and security for manhattan project a graduate of virginia military institute and harvard law school lansdale was commissioned as a second lieutenant in united states army reserve in 1933 he was called up for active duty in june 1941 and was assigned to investigations branch in office of assistant chief of staff g 2 military intelligence of war department general staff he became involved with manhattan project in 1942 eventually becoming brigadier general leslie groves s special assistant for security lansdale coordinated activities of manhattan project s field security teams with those of other agencies such as fbi in april 1945 groves sent lansdale to europe where he worked with alsos mission to secure 1 000 tons of uranium ore from german wirtschaftliche forschungsgesellschaft wifo plant in stassfurt he also participated in planning and execution of operation harborage in which a special allied force went deep behind enemy lines seized 1 5 tons of uranium ingots and captured a number of german nuclear energy project scientists including carl friedrich von weizsacker max von laue karl wirtz horst korsching and erich bagge and otto hahn early life john mannen jack lansdale was born in oakland california on 11 june 1912 son of john lansdale sr and his and dempsey now squire patton boggs initially in cleveland ohio and later in washington d c he married metta virginia tomlinson in trinity episcopal church in houston texas on 17 june 1936 they had five daughters world war ii counterintelligence after graduating from virginia military institute lansdale joined united states army reserve he was commissioned as a second lieutenant in field artillery branch in 1933 and was promoted to first lieutenant in 1937 he was called up for active duty in june 1941 a few months before united states entered world war ii and was assigned to investigations branch in office of assistant chief of staff g 2 military intelligence of war department general staff in february 1942 lansdale now a captain met with james b conant chairman of national defense research committee who briefed him on efforts to create an atomic bomb and charged him with responsibility for counterintelligence at radiation laboratory at university of california berkeley when colonel james c marshal established manhattan district in june 1942 he created a protective security section as part of its staff responsible for security of its people installations and information he also arranged with assistant chief of staff g 2 major general george v strong for support from military intelligence service strong gave lansdale special responsibility for manhattan district and lansdale in turn arranged with g 2s of each of service commands for officers to be assigned to report directly to him thus bypassing both service command g 2s and their commanders he also organised a special counterintelligence group in washington d c headed by himself that reported directly to both strong and brigadier general leslie groves who became director of manhattan project in september 1942 lansdale had security concerns about head of weapons laboratory j robert oppenheimer because of his communist connections but lansdale and groves agreed that oppenheimer was loyal and
