agustin ross cultural centre spanish centro cultural agustin ross previously known as casino ross ross casino is cultural center of city of pichilemu in libertador general bernardo o higgins region chile it was constructed between 1906 and 1909 at request of politician agustin ross edwards structure of cultural center is very similar to that of grand trianon in versailles france it is best known for housing one of first casinos of chile a casino operated in building between 1917 and 1931 after 1931 all casinos except newly opened casino de vina del mar were declared illegal building has also been used as a post office a store selling imported goods a hotel a discotheque and a bar there were two attempts by local government to purchase building first time in 1982 failed while second in 1995 succeeded former casino was declared a national monument of chile along with agustin ross park in february 1988 over time building deteriorated and a project was approved in january 2007 for it to be renovated for use as a cultural center restored building opened in january 2009 and houses local library in january 2010 cultural center was inaugurated by president michelle bachelet and mayor roberto cordova history 1885 1906 idea agustin ross edwards was a wealthy chilean writer member of parliament government minister and politician he reportedly had dream of creating a port which could be an alternative to valparaiso port but this would turn later into creating a touristic place an elite resort collecting most important characteristics of european places which would make it unique for this purpose ross edwards bought fundo pichilemu a farm from juan esteban maturana in 1885 there was a little hotel in fundo pichilemu called la posada which he converted into a first class hotel called gran hotel pichilemu now agustin ross hotel following development of gran hotel pichilemu ross edwards saw need of creating a seaside resort with all comforts and luxuries capable of seducing chilean aristocracy for this purpose he conceived idea of creating a park and a post office building afterwards agustin ross installed amenities such as a laundry stables and stairways among others according to local historian jose arrano acevedo turning pichilemu into a summer resort town for affluent people from santiago and mendoza argentina 1906 1930 construction casino thumb building circa 1910 according to washington saldias gonzalez editor of pichilemu news construction of building lasted from 1906 to 1909 while jorge godoy rojas architect of university of chile states that it was built between 1904 and 1906 ross wanted it to look similar to grand trianon of tuileries palace in paris france he imported building materials and furniture from france and england after its opening building included a post office with telegraph service and a store for imported goods exclusive to clients of gran hotel pichilemu national monuments council asserts that first casino in chile was opened in building in 1906 however historians antonio saldias and jose arrano acevedo have pointed out that it was inaugurated as a de facto casino in summer of 1917 after agustin ross rented building to argentinean businessman alfredo master saldias also noted that there were casinos in chile before agustin ross s arrival in pichilemu in towns such as constitucion casino operated until 1931 when casino de vina del mar first official casino of chile opened all others were declared illegal ross died in 1925 at age of 82 in vina del mar just months before one of his greatest dreams was
