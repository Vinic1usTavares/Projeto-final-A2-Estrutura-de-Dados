mississippi highway 531 ms 531 is a state highway in central mississippi road starts at ms 28 near taylorsville and travels northeastward it crosses from smith county to jasper county and ends at ms 18 in town of bay springs route was designated in 1956 along its current alignment and it was paved by 1960 route description all of ms 531 is located in smith and jasper counties route is legally defined in mississippi code ss 65 3 3 and is maintained by mississippi department of transportation as part of state highway system section in smith county was designated as staff sergeant mark haskin eaton memorial highway in 2011 commemorating a member of army special forces killed in vietnam war around 2 100 vehicles use highway on average each day highway starts at its t intersection with ms 28 east of town of taylorsville road travels northward through forest and turns northeastward at county road 10 b co 10 b it travels to intersection of co 15 and co 33 passing by unincorporated area of old taylorsville ms 531 then continues northeastward to co 99 where it shifts eastward slightly route crosses county line into jasper county and it soon enters corporation limits of bay springs road ends at ms 18 in a t intersection past co 9 history ms 531 was designated in 1956 to a gravel road from ms 20 near taylorsville to ms 18 near bay springs route was paved by 1960 and ms 20 was renumbered to ms 28 highway has not changed significantly since major intersections see also references 531 category transportation in jasper county mississippi category transportation in smith county mississippi
