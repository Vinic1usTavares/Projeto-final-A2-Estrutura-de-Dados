stanislaw wawrzyniec staszic baptised 6 november 1755 20 january 1826 was a leading figure in polish enlightenment a catholic priest philosopher geologist writer poet translator and statesman a pan slavist after 1815 and laissez he supported many reforms in poland he is particularly remembered for his political writings during great four year sejm 1788 92 and for his support of constitution of 3 may 1791 adopted by that sejm he co founded warsaw society of friends of learning precursor to polish academy of sciences of which he became president he served as a member of state council of duchy of warsaw and as minister of trade and industry in congress poland staszic is seen as father of polish geology statistics sociology tatra mountains studies and exploration mining and industry life early life stanislaw staszic was born into a burgher family in town of pila he was baptised on 6 november 1755 youngest of four siblings his father wawrzyniec staszic was mayor of pila and a royal secretary his brothers were antoni 1743 1775 and andrzej 1745 1825 a priest staszic attended secondary school at walcz he studied theology and graduated from a jesuit school at poznan in 1778 and was ordained a catholic priest he took lesser holy orders in 1774 and higher orders about 1778 79 between 1779 and 1781 he continued his studies in france at college de france where he took classes in physics and natural history on returning to poland in 1781 he accepted a position as tutor in house of grand crown chancellor andrzej zamoyski in 1782 he received a doctorate from academy he
