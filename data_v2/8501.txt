up all night is debut studio album by english irish boy band one direction released by syco records in november 2011 in ireland and united kingdom followed by a worldwide release during 2012 four months after finishing third in seventh series of british reality singing contest x factor in december 2010 one direction began recording album in sweden uk and united states working with a variety of writers and producers album is predominantly a pop music album which orientates into pop rock dance pop teen pop and power pop album s lyrical content regards being young relationships heartbreak and empowerment album received generally favourable reviews from contemporary music critics many of whom appreciated album s combination of melodic song craft and catchy pop oriented material that while slickly produced avoided commercial cynicism and adult contemporary posturing of some of their 80s and 90s forebears album bowed at number two on uk albums chart and ultimately became uk s fastest
