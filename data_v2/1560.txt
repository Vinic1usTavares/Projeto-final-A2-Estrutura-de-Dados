australia and first played ice hockey there he moved to czech republic in 2007 in order to further his career and joined junior program of hc vitkovice a member of czech extraliga he first played for senior team in 2011 becoming first australian ice hockey player to play for a professional senior team in europe along with hc vitkovice walker was loaned to several lower level czech teams in 2013 walker signed with youngstown phantoms of junior united states hockey league moving to north america for first time he attended training camp of washington capitals in 2013 and signed with their ahl
