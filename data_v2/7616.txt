chaim topol born september 9 1935 also spelled haym topol mononymously known as topol is an israeli actor singer comedian voice artist film producer author and illustrator he is best known for his portrayal of tevye dairyman lead role in musical fiddler on roof on both stage and screen having performed this role more than 3 500 times in shows and revivals from late 1960s through to 2009 topol began his acting career during his israeli army service in nahal entertainment troupe and later toured israel with kibbutz theatre and satirical theatre companies he was a co founder of haifa theatre his breakthrough film role came in 1964 as title character in sallah shabati by israeli writer ephraim kishon for which he won a golden globe for most promising newcomer male topol went on to appear in more than 30 films in israel and united states including galileo 1975 flash gordon 1980 and for your eyes only 1981 he was described as israel s only internationally recognized entertainer from 1960s through 1980s he won a golden globe for best actor and was nominated for an academy award for best actor for his 1971 film portrayal of tevye and was nominated for a tony award for best actor for a 1991 broadway revival of fiddler on roof he is a founder of variety israel an organization serving children with special needs and jordan river village a year round camp for arab and jewish children with life threatening illnesses for which he serves as chairman of board in 2015 he was awarded israel prize for lifetime achievement early life topol was born on september 9 1935 in tel aviv
