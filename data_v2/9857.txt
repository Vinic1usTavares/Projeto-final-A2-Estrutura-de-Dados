kawan bergeloet perfected spelling kawan indonesian for playmate is a collection of short stories written by soeman hs and first published by balai pustaka in 1941 it contains twelve stories seven of which were previously published in magazine pandji poestaka as well as an introduction by sutan takdir alisjahbana these stories are generally humorous in nature and presented with a diction that shows strong east sumatran influences released in response to commercial success of collection teman doedoek kawan bergeloet has been reprinted several times and received positive critical appraisal soeman together with kasim has since been considered a pioneer of indonesian short story dutch scholar of indonesian literature a teeuw writes that collection is soeman s most interesting contribution to indonesian literature background short stories and sketches in malay have been recorded in indonesia since 1870s and a short story collection h kommer s warna sari was published in 1912 these early stories used vernacular malay and were often humorous or derived from fairy tales or detective fiction short story writing developed further in 1920s and 1930s when short stories and sketches in a more formal register of malay were widely published in such magazines as pandji poestaka and poedjangga baroe first collection of short stories in indonesian literary canon teman doedoek was published by balai pustaka in 1937 this collection was a commercial success selling 4 000 copies by 1941 left thumb soeman hs in 1994 teman doedoek was read by soeman hs a bengkalis born teacher who had already gained popularity as a writer of detective fiction soeman once a student of kasim s had also experimented with more humorous story telling approaches including in his novel pertjobaan setia as well as in numerous short stories he had published through pandji pustaka after commercial success of teman doedoek balai pustaka sought to release a new short story collection thus soeman was contacted which echo folk tale stories publication and reception kawan bergeloet was published by balai pustaka in 1941 with series number 1426 collection s title kawan bergeloet has variously been translated as playmates comrades wrestling and argumentative companions word bergeloet in indonesian can mean either to wrestle or to laugh rosidi identifying bergeloet as meaning to laugh writes that title was meant to indicate that book was intended for entertainment purposes to be read in one s spare time soeman gained recognition as a pioneer of indonesian short story for kawan bergeloet and over subsequent decades was commonly mentioned with kasim in histories of literary form collection has been reissued several times third printing in 1950 introduced an updated spelling as well as nine illustrations by most recent edition was published in 1997 story papan reklame was reprinted in indonesian readings a student reader for indonesian as a foreign language in 1978 rosidi writes that soeman s greatest strength in kawan bergeloet is in his description he considers writer to have avoided cliched descriptions instead using new and original descriptions metaphors and turns of phrase
