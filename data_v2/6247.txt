maximilian johannes maria hubert reichsgraf von spee 22 june 1861 8 december 1914 was a naval officer of german kaiserliche marine imperial navy who commanded east asia squadron during world war i spee entered navy in 1878 and served in a variety of roles and locations including on a colonial gunboat in german west africa in 1880s east africa squadron in late 1890s and as commander of several warships in main german fleet in early 1900s during his time in germany in late 1880s and early 1890s he married his wife margareta and had three children his sons heinrich and otto and his daughter huberta by 1912 he had returned to east asia squadron as its commander and was promoted to rank of vizeadmiral vice admiral following year after outbreak of world war i in july 1914 spee led his squadron across pacific to coast of south america here on 1 november he defeated british 4th cruiser squadron under rear admiral christopher cradock in battle of coronel sinking two of cradock s cruisers and forcing his other two ships to retreat a month later spee decided to attack british naval base in falkland islands but a superior british force surprised him in ensuing battle of falkland islands vice admiral doveton sturdee s squadron which included two powerful battlecruisers destroyed east asia squadron spee and his two sons who happened to be serving on two of his ships were all
