no 114 mobile control and reporting unit is a royal australian air force raaf radar surveillance and air defence unit located at raaf base darwin northern territory it is controlled by no 41 wing under surveillance and response group its role is to prepare for conduct and sustain effective deployable air surveillance and air battle management in unit s designated area of responsibility unit was formed during world war ii and deployed in south west pacific since war it has seen service in malayan emergency konfrontasi between indonesia and malaysia in mid 1960s and war in afghanistan
