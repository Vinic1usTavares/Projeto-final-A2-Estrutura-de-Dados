nenjil or aalayam is a 1962 indian tamil language romantic drama film directed produced and written by c v sridhar film features kalyan kumar devika and r muthuraman in lead roles nagesh manorama and kutty padmini play supporting roles original soundtrack album and background score were composed by viswanathan ramamoorthy while lyrics for songs were written by kannadasan film revolves around a young woman named seetha her husband venu and murali who was seetha s lover due to his tenure overseas seetha s parents force her to marry another person a few years later venu contracts cancer and seetha brings him to chennai to meet a cancer specialist who to her dismay is revealed to be murali realising that something is worrying seetha venu gives her freedom to marry murali after his death remainder of film shows how murali sacrifices his love for sake of couple s happiness and cures venu nenjil or aalayam was released on 26 january 1962 coinciding with republic day in india film received positive critical feedback and went on to become a commercial success songs sonnathu neethaana and engirundhaalum vaazhga attained popularity among tamil diaspora film won national film award for best feature film in tamil and president s award for sridhar at 10th national film awards film became a trendsetter for both fast paced filmmaking and triangular love stories with sacrifice as theme sridhar remade film in hindi as dil ek mandir 1963 and in telugu as manase mandiram 1966 it was also remade in malayalam as hridayam oru kshethram 1976 and in kannada as kumkuma rakshe 1977 former was directed by p subramaniam while latter was by s k a chari plot murali goes abroad to pursue higher studies in medicine during his tenure overseas his girlfriend seetha is forced by her parents to marry another person devastated upon hearing news murali swears a vow of lifelong celibacy and devotes himself to save people suffering from cancer a few years later a man named venu happens to be critically ill with cancer and has to be operated upon treatment for venu progresses well until murali meets venu s wife who is revealed to be seetha when seetha learns that doctor treating venu is murali whom she had left she becomes upset though murali does his best to cure venu seetha is worried that murali might take revenge on her by not providing proper treatment to venu who in meantime learns of murali and seetha s love venu requests murali to marry seetha in case operation is unsuccessful as he does not wish for his wife to become a widow seetha becomes infuriated when she discovers venu s request and tells murali that if venu dies she would die as well murali promises seetha that he will save venu even if he has to risk his own life on line murali works hard to save venu so as not to create a misconception that he killed venu to be with seetha with great difficulty murali manages to successfully cure venu s cancer however when murali reads results of operation he becomes so overjoyed at its success that he unexpectedly dies of high blood pressure due to his over excitement venu and seetha realise that murali had sacrificed his life for their happiness and remains in their hearts woven into story is a subplot following a girl who is in same hospital murali works in and undergoes treatment for same disease that venu contracted however her
