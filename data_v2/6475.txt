wolf journey home originally titled hungry for home a wolf odyssey is a 1997 american young adult novel written by asta bowen originally published by simon schuster with line drawings by jane hart meyer it was retitled and reprinted without illustrations in 2006 by bloomsbury publishing based on true accounts of pleasant valley montana wolf pack novel traces life of a female alpha wolf named marta after forced relocation of her pack in 1989 to an unfamiliar territory terrified marta abandons her pack and begins a journey in search of her home she eventually arrives in ninemile valley where she finds a new mate with whom she starts a new pack bowen was prompted to write story of relocated wolves after becoming outraged over continued poaching of wolves in protected areas of ninemile valley she spent four years researching wolves while working on novel in recounting fates of animals at end she expresses hope in ideals behind efforts to restore wolf populations nominated for a 2006 teens top ten award by american library association novel was praised by critics two praised bowen s ability to avoid anthropomorphism while capturing essence of wolf behavior and life
