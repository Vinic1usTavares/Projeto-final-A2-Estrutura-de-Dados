of australian labor party from 1907 to 1915 fisher was born in crosshouse ayrshire scotland he left school at a young age to work in nearby coal mines becoming secretary of local branch of ayrshire miners union at age of 17 fisher emigrated to australia in 1885 where he continued his involvement with trade unionism he settled in gympie queensland and in 1893 was elected to queensland legislative assembly as a representative of labor party fisher lost his seat in 1896 but returned in 1899 and later that year briefly served as a minister in government of anderson dawson in 1901 fisher was elected to new federal parliament representing division of wide bay he served as minister for trade and customs for a few months in 1904 in short lived government of chris watson fisher was elected deputy leader of labor party in 1905 and replaced watson as leader in 1907 at time labor supported protectionist party minority government of alfred deakin deakin resigned as prime minister in
