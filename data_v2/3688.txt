davenport was at center of a controversy over racist practices at monster a popular gay bar in new york city s west village where she had hosted a weekly show called manster for six years incident began in late september 2018 when bar manager italo lopez sent text messages to davenport s manager dj mitch ferrino indicating that a flyer for manster appeared to be promoting a black night and that this was bad for business lopez also said that two black dancers on flyer needed to be replaced by someone beautiful and that image of davenport herself needed to be smaller after ferrino showed davenport messages she reached out to bar owner charles rice with concerns about racist overtones of lopez s words but rice did not respond following this on september 29 2018 davenport appeared at manster as planned however instead of performing she announced she would no longer be able to work at monster she shared what lopez had said with audience and ended her two minute speech by stating i cannot be a part of this anymore if you don t want my people at party i won t be here shortly thereafter a number of other drag queens canceled their regular shows at monster ferrino also withdrew his weekly event lookqueen which was started in 2014 by bob drag queen from bar lookqueen s acting host cain had urged ferrino to do so rice initially blamed ferrino and davenport for public backlash he experienced calling incident a smear campaign that he ascribed to ferrino having an axe to grind with lopez he further stated that he believed ferrino should not have shown lopez s messages to davenport chalking up their content to a miscommunication stemming from lopez s non native english later on rice apologized and lopez resigned from his position rice arranged for staff at monster to undergo racial sensitivity training but he declined davenport s invitation to have a moderated public discussion about matter personal life heath clark resides in new york city he wed his husband john
