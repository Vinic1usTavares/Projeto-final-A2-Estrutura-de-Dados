and steadily intensified despite only a marginally favorable environment for development and became a category 2 hurricane before making two landfalls on baja california peninsula and mainland mexico hurricane was responsible for significant flooding and storm surges that caused 100 million 2003 usd in damage mostly on peninsula of baja california and resulted in deaths of 12 people marty affected
