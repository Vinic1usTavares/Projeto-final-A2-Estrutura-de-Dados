lazare ponticelli born lazzaro ponticelli 24 december 1897 later as 7 december 12 march 2008 knight of vittorio veneto was at 110 last surviving officially recognized veteran of first world war from france and last of its trenches to die born in italy he travelled on his own to france at age of eight aged 16 he lied about his age in order to join french army at start of war in 1914 before being transferred against his will to italian army following year after war he and his brothers founded piping and metal work company ponticelli freres ponticelli brothers which produced supplies for second world war effort and as of 2017 was still in business he also worked with french resistance against nazis ponticelli was oldest living man of italian birth and oldest man living in france at time of his death every armistice day until 2007 he attended ceremonies honoring deceased veterans in his later years he criticized war and stored his awards from first world war in a shoe box while he felt unworthy of state funeral french government offered him he eventually accepted one however he asked that procession emphasise common soldiers who died on battlefield french president nicolas sarkozy
