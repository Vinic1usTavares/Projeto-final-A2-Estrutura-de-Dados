aspects of their styles remained in his playing but he added greater harmonic freedom of mccoy tyner and others in developing as a hard bop player and then in creating his own style which influenced others from 1980s on miller s style evolved through playing with a series of major jazz figures after leaving university he was pianist with duke ellington orchestra for three years then accompanied
