lazarus is a dystopian science fiction comic book series created by writer greg rucka and artist michael lark two began developing idea in 2012 and partnered with colorist santi arcas to finish art image comics has been publishing book since first issue was released on june 23 2013 other creators were brought in later to assist with lettering and inking a six issue spin off limited series
