philip carteret hill primrose october 23 1864 march 17 1937 was a canadian police officer and fifth lieutenant governor of alberta primrose was born in 1864 in nova scotia he attended pictou academy before graduating from royal military college in 1885 upon his graduation he took up employment with north west mounted police nwmp later merged into royal canadian mounted police over 30 years he spent with nwmp he was stationed at many locations throughout north west territories serving as superintendent on four separate occasions before his retirement from force in 1915 primrose then moved to edmonton and accepted position of city police magistrate a position he would hold for 20 years amongst other positions during world war i he commanded edmonton reserve battalion of canadian army although he had never actively participated in politics in 1936 primrose was appointed lieutenant governor of alberta a position he held for only 167 days before he died after a long illness march 17
