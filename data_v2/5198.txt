formula vol 1 is debut studio album by american singer romeo santos released on november 8 2011 by sony music latin it is santos s first album as a solo artist following break up of american bachata group aventura of which he was lead singer record contains fifteen tracks most of which were composed by santos and co produced with ivan chevere album experiments with sound of bachata and other genres including r b and flamenco it features several anglophone and guest artists including usher tomatito mario domm and lil wayne recording for album took place in 2011 at castle fight klub and emg studios in new york city a deluxe edition of album containing five extra tracks was released exclusively in walmart retail stores in united states in u s formula vol 1 peaked at number one on billboard top latin albums and billboard tropical albums charts and was best selling latin album of 2012 it was certified three times platinum latin field by recording industry association of america riaa for shipping 300 000 copies and had sold 328 000 copies in u s by february 2014 it ranked number thirteen twenty six and seventy seven on argentine mexican and spanish album charts respectively santos promoted record by touring u s latin america and europe it was generally well received by critics who praised production of bachata tracks although some of those with mario domm and mala criticized as obvious attempts to appeal outside of bachata audience album earned santos several accolades including a grammy award nomination three billboard latin music awards a billboard music award a lo nuestro nomination a premios juventud award and a soberano award six singles were released from record four of which you promise mi santa and la diabla reached number one on billboard hot
