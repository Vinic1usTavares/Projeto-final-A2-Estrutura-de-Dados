reginald walter darcy weaver 18 july november 1945 was an australian conservative parliamentarian who served in new south wales legislative assembly for 28 years serving from 1917 in backbenches he entered cabinet of thomas bavin in 1929 as secretary for mines and minister for forests until he returned to opposition in 1930 following success of united australia party in 1932 election weaver returned as secretary for public works and minister for health in stevens ministry in 1935 he was dropped from ministry but was later elected as speaker of new south wales legislative assembly in 1937 holding office until mair government lost power in 1941 weaver then witnessed death of united australia party in 1943 and became leader of new democratic party in 1944 he was then involved in negotiations to form future liberal party which were ultimately successful with weaver becoming first leader of liberal party in april 1945 he served only briefly until dying of a heart attack in
