fallout shelter players build and manage their own vault as an overseer leader and coordinator of their vault players guide and direct citizens of vault known as dwellers and need to keep them happy through meeting their needs such as power food and water they rescue dwellers from wasteland and assign them to different resource generating buildings in vault using special statistics system from other fallout games each character s special profile affects their ability to generate different resources and their statistics can be increased by training them in rooms devoted to each stat dwellers can level up over time increasing their health and can be given new items and weapons to help with various tasks number of dwellers can be increased by waiting for new dwellers from wasteland to arrive or by pairing a male and a female dweller in living quarters to produce babies balancing resources such as food water and power is an important aspect of game many different rooms can be built in vault providing different items or stat bonuses players are not required to spend money in order to accelerate long timers or processes instead having option to instantly complete tasks at risk of catastrophic failures such as fires or infestations players are sometimes rewarded with lunchboxes that contain rewards such as items or resources which can be purchased separately through
