battle of oland was a naval battle between an allied danish dutch fleet and swedish navy in baltic sea off east coast of oland on 1 june 1676 11 june by gregorian calendar denmark and sweden still used julian calendar while dutch republic had switched to gregorian in 1582 83 battle was a part of scanian war 1675 79 fought for supremacy over southern baltic sweden was in urgent need of reinforcements for its north german possessions denmark sought to ferry an army to scania in southern sweden to open a front on swedish soil just as battle began swedish flagship kronan sank taking with it almost entire crew including admiral of realm and commander of swedish navy lorentz creutz allied force under leadership of dutch admiral cornelis tromp took full advantage of ensuing disorder on swedish side acting commander after creutz s sudden demise admiral claes uggla was surrounded and his flagship svardet battered in a drawn out artillery duel then set ablaze by a fire ship uggla drowned while escaping burning ship and with loss of a second supreme commander rest of swedish fleet fled in disorder battle resulted in danish naval supremacy which was upheld throughout war danish
