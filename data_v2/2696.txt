battlefield germany is a turn based strategy video game developed and published by personal software services for commodore 64 released in april 1987 it was also ported to amstrad cpc and zx spectrum later that year it is eighth instalment to strategic wargames series game is set during a fictional scenario in which powers of nato and warsaw pact engage in a conventional war throughout europe mostly centring in west and east germany game is a turn based strategy and focuses on player building and training units that are used to attack opposing side game was released for 8 bit consoles as well as 16 bit consoles battlefield germany received mixed reviews upon release reviewers mainly criticised tempo of gameplay and lack of innovation from original some critics however praised graphics
